Saturday 9th November

Today I didn't even go out as far as the garage! Angela, on the other hand, firstly went to the High Street to buy a newspaper and then took her cake and desert to the church and helped with lunches for the Christmas Fair. Meanwhile, I did the weekly accounts and the plumber arrived early, but left at lunchtime.

When Angela got home at about four o'clock, we spoke with Victoria. They are still having difficulty getting the children to bed.

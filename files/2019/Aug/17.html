Saturday 17th August

After our morning walk before breakfast we spent the rest of the day playing with the children. Brandon and Juniper went off to do the weekly main supermarket shop while I helped Austin build more of his Labo devices. After lunch Brandon helped him and I played with Juniper. Later in the afternoon they persuaded me to go in the pool with them. It was not as warm as I thought!

Victoria and Brandon went to a friend's 50th birthday, so we fed the children and put them to bed (at least by nine o'clock).

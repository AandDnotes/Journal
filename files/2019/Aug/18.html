Sunday 18th August

Once again we started the day, quite early, with a walk before breakfast. It was perhaps a little shorter, but we looked at the new houses for sale nearby.

It was a little cooler today, only 28 degrees C, so we went off in their new 'bus' to the arboretum and walked around part of an artificial lake. We saw turtles and a ground squirrel (chipmonk). After lunch the children made gingerbread men/shapes and later Juniper and I went in the pool. She swam under water with her eyes open!

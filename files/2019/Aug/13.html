Tuesday 13th August

I had been watching the weather forecast to find a time to give the TC a run. I thought I might be able to take it to the WSMGOC meeting last night, but there was a thunder storm not long before I was due to leave! The weather forecast said that today would be fine, but it rained heavily periodically throughout the day.

Angela started cutting the grass after lunch, but it started raining. By about five o'clock it was dry enough for me finish it.

I did some more refinement and checking of my house heat loss calculations and PV panel output over the year. It seems the best we could do is generate 20% of our energy needs across the year, varying from 95% in July and 4% in December and January. I also started looking at electric under floor heating.

Wednesday 9th January

Our second short day. Although we left Oakland 45 minutes late, we arrived at Gatwick only 20 minutes late (at 12:20), after a short night. Russell was there to meet us and we were home before two.

We dumped our bags and then walked to Rosemary avenue to check it and then into Steyning to get some milk. We then unpacked and prepared for an early night.

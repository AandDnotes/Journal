Saturday 26th January

After walking to Beeding to buy a newspaper we only had a little while to read some of it before we had to go to Rosemary Avenue. We had a viewing of our house at one o'clock, so we made some lunch and a flask of coffee and took it to Rosemary avenue to eat. We had decided to bring the TV back here, since there is no longer a licence. I had expected to find the TV box in the loft, but there is nothing up there. While un-connecting the TV Angela vacuumed the hall and dining room. We also brought back the DVD player.

Before leaving Rosemary Avenue we called on Sandy and John to see their new extension. John demonstrated his new 'in wall' speakers and I helped him move a heavy cupboard.

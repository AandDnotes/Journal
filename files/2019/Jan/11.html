Friday 11th January

Another late morning. By the time that we had eaten breakfast and showered it was almost 10:30. Angela walked to Steyning while I read the surveyor's report and caught up with email. In between I repacked the stop tap, which was leaking badly, syphoned off more fuel from the TC and took it to Simms, who poured it into a drum. Meanwhile Angela went to the veg shop to get some Seville oranges. On our way home we called in at Rosemary Avenue to read the gas and electricity meters.

Angela cooked the oranges while I did the weekly accounts, monthly accounts and shares update.

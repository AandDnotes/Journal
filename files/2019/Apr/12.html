Friday 12th April

Another bright day, but a bit colder, with an easterly wind. So we did our sorting/packing indoors. After going to the doctor's to talk about her cholesterol level, Angela worked on the dining room cupboard and I worked in the study. Before that I put an ad on Friday Ad for our wardrobes. Then I cleared both the corner shelves and the lorry, but only removed the former from the wall.

In the evening we went to the Fish Factory and used our voucher from Jacob Steel.

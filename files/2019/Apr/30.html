Tuesday 30th April

We did it! We moved from Kingsmead Close to Penlands Vale. The removers arrived before eight and we finally locked the door at five to one after eating our lunch standing up in the garden. It was, I think, about four o'clock before the removers left. Our only real problem is that the space for the dish washer is too narrow! So our machine is in the garage and we will have to decide if we need to buy a narrower model. It was all hectic and very tiring. We walked down to the White Horse for a meal.

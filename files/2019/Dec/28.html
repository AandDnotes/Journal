Saturday 28th December

This morning was a surrogate Thursday in that we went to Sainsbury's to do some grocery shopping. Angela had been to the High Street beforehand, mainly to go to the butcher, while I showered.

The afternoon was spent doing odds and ends, like putting the cases back in the loft, making a temporary fix to Angela's glasses and doing the weekly accounts. This morning I tried to remove the ceramic disk cartridge int eh cold kitchen tap, but I couldn't free it. I was afraid that I might loosen something else, so we will need to get a plumber to replace it.

Saturday 7th December

The plumber has finished the bathroom! Now I just need to complete it's paining. He arrived at 8:30 and left a little before twelve. Someone should call on Monday to pick up the rubbish.

Meanwhile, Angela went to the church to cook the turkey rolls for Monday's lunch club. I did the weekly accounts. After lunch we went down again to carve the turkey, which only took an hour. When we got home we spoke with Victoria.

Once again this is just a note of the events of the day, rather than my thoughts and commentary on the world around me.

Sunday 1st December

A fine day, even if I didn't see much of it. While Angela went to church I put the first coat of primer on the bathroom ceiling and walls, although it took until one o'clock to get the first coat on. After lunch I got a second coat on the ceiling before we spoke to Paul et al and then managed to get a second coat on the walls before dinner. I had to clean the brush and rollers after dinner. So now it is almost ready for painting!

Paul had all the family round, since Wednesday is Sinterklaus's birthday and the children get presents from him. Angela spent a lot of time writing letters and Christmas cards. Her deafness is pretty bad. Even if she is looking at me I need to shout.

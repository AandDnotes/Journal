Tuesday 25th June

I spent the morning and a little of the afternoon cleaning and lubricating the windows and doors. I couldn't get the locking mechanism on the top window in our bedroom to move, even after lubrication.

Two 'window fixers' called to look at the sliding patio door (that doesn't slide very well) and also looked at the bedroom window. I then fixed two screw eyes to the wall in the front garden so that Angela could tie up a bush.

Then it was quick paint of waterproofer on the seams of the TC's hood, before I replaced the spark plug in the strimmer. It then started first pull! So I strimmed the twitten along our fence and then removed some weeds on the drive.

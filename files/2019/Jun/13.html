Thursday 13th June

Basically a standard Thursday, but with a few tweaks. We started by taking two mirrors to the charity warehouse and buying a bag of block paving sand from Wickes, before going to Victoria Grand. From there I picked up a free bottle of wine from Majestic (courtesy of Barclays) and then we had coffee in Morrisons before going on to Lyons Farm. I popped into Boots to speak to the pharmacist about my itchy foot. She thought it was fungal and so suggested a course of hydrocortisone, followed, if necessary, by Lamisil.

We were home in reasonable time, but there was no reply from the person who said they wanted the pew. So I spent most of the afternoon sending email and sorting out things for the IET social.

The big news of the day was that contracts have finally been exchanged on Rosemary Avenue, with completion in a weeks time.

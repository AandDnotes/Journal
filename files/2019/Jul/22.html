Monday 22nd July

I was still in my dressing gown when I realised that I should be leaving to go to Horsham to meet with Gregg and later Norman. I was about ten minutes late, but we had time to review the the Sussex Network budget submission. When Norman arrived we reviewed the 'social', which was generally agreed to be a success, apart from the number of members attending. I was home at midday.

Angela was still out, but when she got home said that she had met Gwen, who was searching for her husband (who has dementia). While I made lunch Angela went out to help try to find him. He was found on his way home.

After lunch we went to Victoria Grand to visit Dad and then went into Worthing to look at the dining room furniture options. I also bought a shirt.

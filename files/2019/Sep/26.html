Thursday 26th September

We got off fairly early start to visit Dad at Victoria Grand and were able to show him a photo of Alan's new puppy. So we got to Sainsbury's early to do the weekly groceries shop and therefore were home earlier than usual.

After an early lunch Angela went to have her hair cut and then I went off to Brighton for a Gift Oversight Group meeting. It did not take long and I was home before four o'clock. When I got back we decided that the fridge/freezer was broken. I powered it off and on in case something needed to be reset, but no joy. So we went to Storrington to see if we could get a replacement. However, it seems that the same hight fridge/freezer is no longer available. They are either lower or too high for the available space.

After looking at the options we decided to bring the old fridge in from the garage and buy a small (table top) freezer to stand on top. So we ordered one from Curry's, for delivery on Monday. We also paid them to remove the old fridge/freezer. When the kitchen is revamped we will probably install an integrated fridge/freezer.

Thursday 19th September

Today was busy, with us trying to fit two day's worth of errands in one day, since the Panda goes in for a service and MoT tomorrow. We were fairly early at Victoria Grand and then called into Morrisons to buy plants on our way to Lyons Farm. I printed a couple of photos and we did the weekly groceries shop.

So after lunch we did Friday's trips. We started at Dunelm, went on to Halfords and B&Q, then went to the tip and finally Lidl before coming home. We bought some plate racks for the sideboard and a shade for the lamp on the sideboard. Then it was brake fluid and a water butt before we dumped the bird bath from the front garden and the oil from the TC. Angela bought some items from Lidl for the church homeless collection.

When we got home I installed the the water butt in the walled garden. Now we just need to see if it fills when it rains.

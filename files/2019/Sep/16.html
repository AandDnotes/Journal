Monday 16th September

Angela needed to leave early after lunch for her WI meeting, so I went to Victoria Grand on my own, in the TC. The object was to drain the engine oil while it was still hot. So when I got back I removed the sump plug and the oil filter. Leaving the final drops to drip into a plastic tub I put the car up on axle stands in preparation for the rest of the service jobs.

The speaker at Angela's WI meeting talked about sleep (and the lack of it), so I scanned part of the article in Saturday's paper and sent it to Victoria.

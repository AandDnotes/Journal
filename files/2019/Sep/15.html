Sunday 15th September

Another fine, warm day. While Angela went to church I removed the wire netting from the top of the wall in the rose garden.

Angela worked in the garden and I found that the 'lift the dot' on the driver's door had been fitted upside down. So I refitted it and replaced the pin with one with a longer screw that, hopefully, will grip the plastic plug more firmly.

As usually on a Sunday we spoke with Paul and Rowan.

Sunday 1st September

We had a quieter day today. After a late breakfast we walked round to the nearest playground, let the children play and then had a game of frisbee. Although it was still early, we drove into downtown Davis to the 'Black Bear Diner' for lunch. It was very busy (we had a half hour wait for a table) and the food was not very good, although the children enjoyed it. So it was quite late when we got home.

We then did things inside and out, including lego building, before the children went in the pool. After a 'cheese and biscuits' supper Juniper took herself to bed early and went to sleep, Austin stayed up until about nine, watching Brandon play a computer game.

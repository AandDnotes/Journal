Saturday 30th March

Today was Paul's 40th birthday and, at least here, it was like a summer's day with sunshine from dawn to dusk. However, we really only did three things today. We started by taking a car load of stuff to the Shoreham tip and then went to Rosemary Avenue to finish cutting the grass and to do a little cleaning of cupboards and window sills.

Then, apart from putting the cover over the TC, we spoke with Victoria et al. It was the end of their school spring break and Austin had been to a Lego club each morning. Before dinner I did manage to do a little more of the conversion of my 'outings' web pages to the grid-based version.

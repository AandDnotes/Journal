Wednesday 6th March

I spent most of the day at Mike Wooldridge's house, mainly in his workshop, painting the inside of 15 pieces of trunking with blackboard paint. As I did the last one Mike pointed out that I needed to do under the top edges as well! Mike's wife, Chris, gave me lunch. As usual, Angela went to table tennis after her lunch.

We had a relatively early dinner and then walked down to the Steyning Centre to see the film 'Kings of Thieves'. It was very entertaining.

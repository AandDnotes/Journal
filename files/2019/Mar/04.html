Monday 4th March

The day started quite bright, but during the afternoon we had some shar, heavy showers, including som hail. It didn't feel particularly warm either. Angela had a Lunch Club AGM this morning and so I went to Victoria Grand on my own. I showed Dad the photo of Pippin (and some of the others that he had already seen) and got home at about 11:30. Angela was back for lunch, as the treasurer for the lunch club!

Between showers we went to Rosemary Avenue to collect the nest of tables and some paintings that we wanted, plus a pedal bin. I scanned the 'authority to exchange' form and sent it to Angela Payne. Then I worked further on my revised 'outings' web page.

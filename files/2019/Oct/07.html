Monday 7th October

Another quiet, but dry day. Rowan went to school, but was home for lunch and then back by 3:30. I helped Paul remove some old drinking troughs from the stalls in the stable and later we cleaned the rabbit's indoor cage, ready for them to come inside for the winter.

Greet and Wim came for coffee (and biscuits) and to say goodbye this afternoon. They found out that we will be back for Christmas!

Tuesday 1st October

We started by going into Worthing so that Angela could pay in her coffee morning cash. We also bought some Euros for our trip to Paul's.

After lunch Angela went to a funeral service and later we collected the frozen food from the church freezer, packing it into the tiny freezer that had been on for 24 hours.

Then it was time to drive to Crawley for an IET committee meeting. It started raining heavily just as I got to Crawley. Then it rained heavily most of the way home.

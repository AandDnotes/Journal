Friday 4th October

Luckily the men coming to clear the gutters and fit a mesh did not arrive until 9:30, so we had a bit of a lie-in. We then spent most of the morning playing games with Rowan (which she generally won). It rained nearly all morning.

After lunch the weather improved and they went to the supermarket to buy things for cake making while I played with Flo in the garden. When they got back they baked a lemon drizzle cake and we helped Rowan make some cake decorations.

Paul was late home since it was his company's monthly Friday evening get-together, when the boss gives them an update.

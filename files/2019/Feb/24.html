Sunday 24th February

It was another sunny day with practically no wind. Although there was frost on the roofs first thing, it soon warmed up.

Angela went off to church and stayed for a meeting where lunch was served. I worked on the March IET newsletter. I finally finished it after dinner and sent out test versions, but I forgot to add the links to the registration buttons.

After lunch I went into the garden and finished pruning the apple tree. Then, when Angela got home, we spoke to Rowan (or a tiger). They had been to the zoo and she had her face painted.

Thursday 28th February

The last day of February and the weather has changed. It started grey, with a little sunshine, then rained heavily and then back to grey.

We started by visiting Dad at Victoria Grand. He has had a 'flip-tap' fitted and his 'bag' removed, at least for some of the time.

We went on to Holmebush, since Angela had a 20% off voucher for M&S. The journey took a long time due to road works. Angela bought a jumper and I bought two pairs of trousers. We then did the grocery shopping.

After a late lunch we sorted the books in one of the book cases. So now we have several cardboard boxes full of books to take to a charity shop.

Monday 27th May

Spring Bank Holiday and a fine, but breezy day. We started by visiting Dad at Victoria Grand and then called into B&Q on the way home. For some reason the traffic was chaotic around Lyons Farm. All we wanted was some neoprene tubing and some water retention granuals!

After an early lunch Angela went down to the Steyning centre to help with the 'teas' as part of the Steyning Spring Fair. I emptied the box of paper in the garage and started experimenting with making a 'wing bracket' from some wire that was in the garage when we arrived. Then I walked down to the fair and had a cup of tea at the Steyning Centre. Angela was washing up!

When I got home I finished making four 'brackets' before dinner.

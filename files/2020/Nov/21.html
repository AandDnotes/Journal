Saturday 21st November

It was dry this morning, if overcast and breezy. So we took our usual walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper and rolls. We then took the long route home via the lower horseshoe, the first walk this week.

I then cleaned out the front gutter. It had no leaves in it, but there were quite a few sycamore seeds around the top of the down pipes. We also moved the wheelie bins to the side of the garage.

After lunch it was time to do the weekly accounts before we spoke with Victoria, which took two hours!

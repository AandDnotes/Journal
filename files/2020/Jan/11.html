Saturday 11th January

We spent most of the morning, from ten until 1:30, at Freestyle in Broadwater, helping Kevin come up with a design for our kitchen. We thought that his estimate of one to one and a half hours was long, but even after three and a half hours we had only come up with a first draft. At least we have prints of the design (and he emailed images later of the design with a few embellishments). However, we forgot about waste bins and the boiler will need to be moved (or replaced and put in a slightly different position) for that part of the design to work. We also need to decide whether or not to have the window replaced before the kitchen.

After our late lunch Angela went to the Methodist Church to their beetle drive. Meanwhile I spoke briefly to Victoria before it became too dark to get in the washing.

Thursday 23rd January

Not only was it a busy day, but we spent (or committed to spend) a lot of money. We started as usual, by going to Victoria Grand to visit Dad and then on to Sainsbury's to do the weekly groceries shop.

Usually that is the end of events on Thursdays. But today, before I went to the hygienist and then for a check-up with the dentist, we looked at the quote from Howdens and I made an appointment with Freestyle to place an order for a new kitchen. That will require a deposit as well. I then accepted the quotation from British Gas for a new boiler, paying a deposit in the process.

As I was completing that, the man from Everest arrived to talk about windows. I was determined that I wouldn't place an order today and even told him that. Nevertheless we did just that, paying yet another deposit. I feel that I was coerced into placing an order by a good sales technique, even though it was the right thing to do. Their product and service are undoubtedly good and the price is not that much higher than I had estimated. Nevertheless it doesn't feel quite right.

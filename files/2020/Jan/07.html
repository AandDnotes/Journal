Tuesday 7th January

After a late start Angela went to the High Street, mainly to go to the butcher. I mostly worked on my list of publications, although I did manage to get the circlip off the coat cupboard door handle and remove the spring. I ordered a pack of six of the same size, but couldn't find circlips that were small enough. Angela started making the first batch of marmalade and also cooked three cakes (as well as dinner).

Tuesday 14th January

Angela took her lunch club cash to the bank in Storrington, by bus. It was raining and she got quite wet. Much to my surprise the door handle springs that I had ordered arrived in the post. So I fitted one to the coat cupboard door handle.

After lunch it was still raining, but I went to Field Place for an RCEA talk on Operation Overlord. He was a very good speaker and an interesting talk. It was still raining at the end! When I got home I 'played' with a method of creating an html timeline.

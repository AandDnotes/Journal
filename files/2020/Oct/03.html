Saturday 3rd October

Luckily we missed the worst of the rain today and it was very calm, even when raining heavily. We walked to the High Street to buy a newspaper and some rolls. and we only needed an umbrella to keep off the light rain. It stopped raining for a while in the afternoon and so I cleared the front gutter, which was quite blocked at both ends.

Otherwise the day was spent doing the weekly and monthly accounts and updating Greg's draft document to be sent to our industrial partners.

As we were just starting to wash up the dinner things, Victoria called. They had been out to look at electric cars and were planning to go back and buy a Chevrolet Bolt.

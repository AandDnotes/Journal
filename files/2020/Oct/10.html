Saturday 10th October

We started with some exercise, going to the newsagents and then on around the lower horseshoe. A bank statement arrived yesterday, so when I updated the weekly accounts I reconciled them against the statement.

Before doing the accounts someone called to collect five cardboard boxes. They had advertised on FreeCycle for packing boxes.

After lunch we spoke with Victoria. They seem to have survived a full week of going to school. Brandon was due to return today from Arkansas, where he was taking the place of a breeder who had left. He stayed an extra night and Gene drove down to meet him. Arkansas is the state beneath Missouri.

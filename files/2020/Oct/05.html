Monday 5th October

A much better day, weather-wise. It was dry and later in the morning quite bright, almost sunny. It wasn't until later in the afternoon that we got a few spots of rain.

This morning I firstly went to Mike's at Woodmancote to collect the two boxes of Control Technologies equipment and then, after coffee, delivered them to Hamish in Eastergate. I just got back in time to prepare lunch. Angela had been working in the garden.

Yesterday the recycling collection men managed to pull out (and loose) one of the plugs that hold the lid on to the bin. The other was partially out also. So I cut a couple of inches off an old broom handle (the one that Angela uses for walking, although I don't think she will miss a couple of inches), reduced it in diameter, with a bit of a taper and hammered it into the bin. The bin is otherwise in good ccondition, although our general waste bin has a split in one side.

I discovered that my attempts to add the Exchange server to my MacBook Air had removed it from the iMac! When I tried to go into Outlook in the browser I had to change my password, but it didn't seem to let me use it to add a server to Mail for about an hour. However, all is now back to normal.

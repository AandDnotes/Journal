After lunch I printed a good drawing of an SU carburettor jet assembly and then went into the garage. I removed both needles to check that they were the right type and checked that they were not bent. I then sorted through one of the gasket packs I'd bought from the Octagon Club and stripped down the rear carburettor. In the process I found that the choke on the rear carb was no coming fully off. This might account for the sooty rear plugs and the high consumption figures.

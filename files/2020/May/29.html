Friday 29th May

Another hot, sunny day. As usual we were late getting going, so Angela 'pottered' in the garden, planting pots and baskets, and I 'played' with my RF equipment. I managed to get it working (the main problem was the connections to the servos) and one servo did not respond to commands, but was erratically uncontrolled. I verified that the correct data was being received and, mostly the transmission was working. However, when I decided to video the equipment with just one servo working, that servo stopped!

After an early dinner we walked around the lower horseshoe path, starting by going across the cricket field.

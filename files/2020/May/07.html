Thursday 7th May

We were fairly early for our weekly visit to the supermarket. This week there was a short queue, but again I sat in the car and phoned Dad. They are still unable to get any testing kits, but everyone in Victoria Grand is well. I then read until Angela came out and we were home by eleven.

After lunch we walked our extended round the block. It was a very warm and sunny day today. I then put photos taken on our walks into a web page. I just need to decide where to put it.

This week, at eight o'clock, Brian next door played 'We'll Meet Again' on his trumpet.

My loaf again sank in the middle, even though it rose up to the window and I used only white and wholemeal flour (not including any brown). The only difference is the yeast.

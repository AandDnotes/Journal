Thursday 21st May

Weekly groceries shopping day. I sat in the car while Angela queued. She wasn't that long, but I had time to phone Dad and read most of a 'free' magazine, although I had to sit in the passenger seat since it was too sunny in the driver's seat. We were home just after eleven.

The forecast said it would not be as hot as yesterday, although it seemed hotter, so we didn't sit for long in the garden drinking coffee. Angela phoned people and I modified my Adestra email and re-submitted it.

After lunch Angela pottered in the garden and I cleared the gutters. Then I washed the gutters and facias along the front. I still have the back of the garage and the rest of the house to wash.

Brian next door played his trumpet as usual at eight o'clock as we clapped at the front door.

Sunday 31st May

Another hot, sunny day. However, we had a day off from our walks. Angela did more pottering in the garden, although I think she has now planted out everything she has. I decided to add a switch to the power input to the oscilloscope, but then it wouldn't work at all. I removed the switch, but still no luck. It seems that the power gets as far as the first diode, but no further. I can measure a stable forward voltage on the diode, but I can only assume it has failed. I spent some time trying to find a two-pin plug that would fit the alternative power input, which may be easier than the cylindrical connector.

As usual we spoke with Paul and Rowan. They were sat outside. Tomorrow is another holiday for them.

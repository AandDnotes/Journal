Saturday 9th May

Another hot, sunny day. We again took the long route to the High Street to go to the bakers, butchers and the Co-op (for grapes and a newspaper). We got home about eleven. The rest of the morning was spent dealing with email and doing the weekly accounts.

Angela again pottered in the garden and later we had a three-way FaceTime call with Victoria and paul. The video and sound quality was very good. They tried to set up a drawing game, but only Rowan and Austin could join. They seemed to enjoy it.

Just as we were starting the call so our new mower arrived. When we finished the call I assembled the mower and charged the battery. After dinner I finished mowing the walled garden grass and nearly finished the front grass before the battery ran out! It should last for twice the size of our total grass area. So I charged it for a short while and then finished off the mowing. Later I registered the mower, charger and battery, needing information from the 'plates' on all three items.

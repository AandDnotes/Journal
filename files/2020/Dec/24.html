Thursday 24th December

A bright, but chilly Christmas Eve. In fact it was really too cold to go out. The furthest I went was to the recycling bin. However, I did print two bingo cards and made two sets of counters. Apparently Rowan does not seem enthusiastic, but we are prepared if she want to play.

In the afternoon we had a FaceTime call with Kate and Dick. The connection kept breaking, but it was good to talk with them.

Saturday 26th December

A Boxing Day of mixed weather. It started dry and reasonably warm, but breezy. By lunch-time it was raining and gales are forecast over night. Luckily, while Angela went to the High Street to get some milk, I started fixing our new house numbers on the front wall. Since the bricks are quite 'bumpy' it was difficult to get the holes in the right place. I just hope that Angela doesn't notice that the number one is not quite vertical.

After that I cleaned out the the rear gutters and cleaned the roff over the back door. Hopefully they will cope with the coming storm and the extra load from Lynn's garage roof.

Angela cooked a chocolate roulade, but otherwise we just did some of the jigsaw.

Tuesday 1st December

A sunny day, but the sun doesn't reach our house until 10 o'clock and then is gone by four.

Angela went off to the Grammar School to pick up the copies of her WI programme and I went to the Post Office to post Victoria's birthday card and buy Christmas stamps. There was quite a queue.

After lunch I cleared the weeds from the drive before it got too cold. However, the grass between the blocks remains.

We had an early dinner, because I was hosting an IET lecture webinar. It seemed to go very well.

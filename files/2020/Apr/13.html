Monday 13th April

Easter Monday, although one that Covid-19 has shut down. It was also a very different day, weather-wise, from yesterday. It started cloudy, about ten degrees cooler than yesterday and with a brisk northerly, biting wind. Nevertheless we started the day with a walk. We took our usual extended block route, investigating a path higher up the Bostal on the way. It seemed to go higher and in the wrong direction, so we turned around.

Angela spent the rest of the morning cooking cakes and biscuits, while I phoned Dad and finished off porting another TC article to the web site.

After lunch I went in the garage, fitted the brake drums, put some wax under the wings and cleaned and fitted one of the wheels.

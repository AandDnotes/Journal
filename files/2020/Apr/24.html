Friday 24th April

We took a different route for our walk this morning, going across to the Rifle Range and up the hill, then down to Mouse Lane. We were out for an hour.

When we got home I wrote notes on my chat with the Chair of the Solent Network and sent it to our committee members. Meanwhile Angela pottered in the front garden.

After lunch I went into the garage and re-assembled the rear number plate back-plate and rear lights. Angela again pottered in the garden.

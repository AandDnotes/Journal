Friday 10th April

Probably hotter than yesterday and sunny all day. So we started by going for a walk, this time an extended trip around the block. At the top of Newham Lane we went up a path that joins the path around the upper horseshoe. Overall it took about 45 minutes. We were glad that we went out then, since it got hotter later.

At midday we had a FaceTime call with Kate and Dick. We should have been visiting them today for the weekend. We spent about an hour and a half talking with them.

So after a latish lunch we went outside, Angela in the garden and me in the garage. Angela potted up cuttings and I vacuumed the garage, clearing most of the dead leaves and sycamore seeds, but mainly to remove dust before I put paint on the brake drums and backplates.

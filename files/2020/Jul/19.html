Sunday 19th July

Angela had a Zoom church session at ten o'clock, so I went into the garage. Before lunch I checked one wheel, the near-side front. It was fine inside the brake drum, but there was a slight weep from the brake pipe joint with the cylinder. I tired tightening it with a few strikes of a hammer on the end of the spanner. After lunch I checked the off-side front and off-side rear drums, both were fine.

During the afternoon we talked with Paul and showered after dinner so as to be early in the morning when we are expecting the electrician.

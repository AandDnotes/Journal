Then it was off to Sainsbury's. I got some cash (walking slowly) while Angela did the shopping. It was about 11:30 when we got home. Angela drove, since I couldn't lift my right leg.

I spent the afternoon writing email to our student and to Julie. I also started drafting one to teh IET Sussex committee.

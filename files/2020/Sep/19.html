Saturday 19th September

As is often the case on Saturdays, we took the long route to the High Street via the 'snake' path. We only wanted a newspaper, but all the Telegraphs had gone, so I bought a Times. It started cool, but was warm enough to wear just a sweater and was hazy sunshine for most of the day.

For me, the rest of the day was spent making notes for this month's newsletter. Apart, that is, from the hour and a half that we spent talking with Victoria. She wanted to talk earlier than usual, since they were going to test drive a car; a new one for Brandon to use. It did mean that we didn't start getting our lunch until after two o'clock.

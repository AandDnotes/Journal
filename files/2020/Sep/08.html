Tuesday 8th September

We had a good day. We left at ten, stopped at Cobham for a coffee and arrived at Langley Park just after midday. Kate and Dick had arrived a few minutes earlier. We talked, had lunch, went for a walk, had tea and cake and left just before 6:30. That meant that we were home just before eight. The weather started overcast but became brighter as the day went on.

When we got home we discovered that our new neighbours had moved in opposite.

Saturday 7th March

A busy morning and a lazy afternoon. We started as usual with a walk into the village to buy a newspaper. I then removed old TV arial cables coming into the sitting room and our bedroom. To do this I climbed up on to the scaffold at the back.

We then took Reenie and Joy down to the Methodist Church to the St Barnabus Cottage Pie lunch. We were home by 2:30 and then spoke with Victoria just after four.

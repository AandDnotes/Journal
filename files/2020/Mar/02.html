Monday 2nd March

Quite a busy day and a dry one, with a bit of sunshine. On our way to Victoria Grand we called into the flooring shop and borrowed a larger sample of the style of floor that we are thinking of for the hall, dining room and kitchen. We also gave them the target days for screeding the floor.

On the way back we went to Roger's Farm to have coffee and buy some cuttings compost.

After lunch Angela went in the garden and I downloaded the list of registrants for tomorrow's talk (all 112 of them) and produced an attendance list. Later, my speaker sent his biography.

The brake cylinder seals arrived in the post, so later I reassembled the rear off-side slave cylinder and fitted it to the back plate after cleaning it. The new seals were fine, but, as before, the boots seemed too small. However, with a little brake fluid as lubricant, I was able to 'prise' the boot over the piston and over the cylinder.

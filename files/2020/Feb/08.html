Saturday 8th February

We had an unexpected little outing this morning. We walked to the High Street to buy a newspaper, but nowhere had one with the magazine. So we walked to Beeding, who had a complete newspaper. We stopped on the way back at the Castle for a coffee (cheap until midday) before continuing home. This took most of the morning, since it was 11:15 before we were home and we needed an early lunch since someone was calling to measure up for flooring in the kitchen, hall and dining room.

In fact he arrived half an hour early and it did not take long for him to measure the rooms. Angela then went to the garden centre and I filled the holes in the boiler cupboard, including trying to patch up the area around the flue outlet. I also did the weekly accounts, including reconciliation against the statement.

Later we spoke to Victoria, with a brief look at Juniper's and Austin's short haircuts and a description from Brandon of his first week in his new job.

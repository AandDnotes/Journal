Saturday 22nd February

After a walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper and some bread rolls I spent the rest of the morning doing the weekly accounts and a few other jobs in the study. After lunch I went into the garage to try bleeding the rear near-side brake cylinder, trying to replace the fluid in the process. Quite a bit went on the floor, both due to the bleeding tool coming apart at the slave cylinder and overfilling the master cylinder.

In between my attempts at bleeding the brakes we spoke with Victoria. Brandon is still trying to get confirmation that he can take time off during August, so they haven't yet booked their air tickets.

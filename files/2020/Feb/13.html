Thursday 13th February

A normal start for a Thursday with a visit to Dad at Victoria Grand, although we went via the A24 since there was a lot of water around. Then it was off to do the weekly groceries shop at Lyons Farm.

Since I was going to an IET events meeting in the evening we had our main meal at lunchtime. Then I checked and lubricated the Energy Watch turbines and packed the car with the kit needed for tomorrow.

Then it was off to the events meeting at Bernard's. Unexpectedly he provided a variety of curries! We spent a little too long eating since it was eleven o'clock before I got home!

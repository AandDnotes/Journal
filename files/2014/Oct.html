23 October 2014
Thursday

I had planned our journey to Rotterdam quite carefully. However, I had not allowed for the Jubilee line being suspended while we were on a train at Southwark station! We were given poor advice by a member of staff, to walk to the next station and continue with tube and DLR. Inside the Central Station we met Krista and Rowan and then used the Metro and bus to get to Krimpen ann de Lek. 24 October 2014
Friday

We woke up as Paul closed the front door. Then, after a breakfast of porridge and toast, we went off to view a house with Krista and Rowan. We met Greet there also. The house was in the same village as the 'project' house, but was a relatively new house, with a wooden house on the front and a 'studio' apartment made out of a concrete silo! The inside of the main house was quite spectacular and there were many outbuildings, including a stable and a large workshop. Many of the buildings contained birds and other animals, and four horses were in the field.
Afterwards we drove past the 'project' house and then on to Greet and Wim's for coffee. Since it was raining and very misty we stayed there for lunch and afternoon tea before returning to Krista's flat.

25 October 2014

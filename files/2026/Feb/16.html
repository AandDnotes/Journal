Monday 16th February

A tiring day. It was mainly bright and dry, with only a shower in the afternoon. Angela went to her Monday Club while I uploaded my video of Tuesday's IET talk to YouTube.

The tubing that I'd ordered for David F-B's tape deck arrived, so I cut a piece, but found that, althought eh ID was good, the OD was a bit to big. So I put it on the capstan and rubbed it on some emery paper to reduce the diameter. It then seemed to fit the hole with enough clearance. Whether it will be even enough we have yet to see. I had trouble getting the deck re-assembled, since the transfer wheel slider had jumped out of place. I got the deck back together before lunch, but didn't have a chance to fit it into the music centre.

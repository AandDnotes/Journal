Saturday 14th February

The radio and newspaper kept reminding us that it was 'Valentines Day'; not something that we take any notice of. We started by going to the High Street for a newspaper and some rolls.

After lunch I went to the Repair Cafe. It was busy. We had a queue of items on the electrical bench and I brough home a vacuum cleaner that needs a new cable fitting.

When I got home we had a FaceTime call with Victoria. We then spent most of the evening watching the Winter Olympics, even though we are not that interested.

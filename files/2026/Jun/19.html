Friday 19th June

I put the B's top down and took it for my test figure of eight run. It took me a while to get it into gear, since I had to put the clutch pedal to the floor! So later I researched a little about replacing the clutch.

Then we took the B off to the Crown Inn in Dial Post to meet up with other WSMGOC members for a pub lunch. The food was good, but we each had to work out how much we had spent, since the pub couldn't, for some reason, do that! We got home a little before three.

I had found that the clock in the B is a little quartz movement, but with a flat battery. So I replaced it and now have a working clock. Then I took the drill from the Repair Cafe apart and researched any simple fixes. I also took the garage back door lock off, lubricated it and screwed it back on, tightly.

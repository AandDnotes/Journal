Thursday 18th June

After doing the weekly shop at Lyons Farm we went into Worthing, firstly to have coffee in Waitrose and buy a few things and secondly to go to 'Hidden Hearing' to get filters for Angela's hearing aids.

Unfortunately, it rained for a lot of the afternoon. So I didn't take the B for a run. I had wanted to try it out after the the recent work and to test out the gearbox, which seems to work better when it has been used.

While Angela went to a St Barnabas afternoon tea, I replied to a question from Patrick R about the brakes on the TC. He has already done about 600km in the TC since he bought it and now they are planning to go on a rally to Sicily (about 1,500km) in September!

Later I updated the IET Sussex web page.

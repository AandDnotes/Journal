Tuesday 2nd June

First thing this morning I tried to log into Angela's Nationwide online account. It asked for information this time, but kept telling me that one or both were wrong. So we decided to go into Worthing to a branch in the hope that we could sort it out, or more importantly, move the matured ISA to a new one.

The lady behind the counter moved the money to a new ISA and found that the code number had been locked. So she asked for a new one to be sent to us. A very successful trip. To celebrate we had lunch in Waitrose.

At four o'clock I joined an IET workgroup session with members from around the world. It was scheduled to finish at seven, but we finished at 6:30.

Later we ordered a Cornish cream tea for Kate, for her birthday.

Monday 1st June

The first day of meteorological summer and my 78th birthday. But the weather was is more like a spring day. I only had one card, from Angela.

Angela went to her Monday Club and I emailed a contact at Rampion 2, asking if he could give a talk to the IET. I also updated the IET Sussex home page to include photos of our last two STEM events. I also made a start on doing the monthly accounts and Angela cut the grass after 'no mow May'.

Later I got a call from Tony at Simms. It seems that after Doug had fitted the new clutch slave cylinder, he couldn't get the master cylinder to work. So I ordered a new one. The MGOC didn't have any in stock (they were on order), but Rimmer Bothers had a choice. I decided that the original, Lockheed metal reservoir version was too expensive. However, an aftermarket version, with a plastic reservoir, was less than half the price. I phoned them and ordered the aftermarket version. I also managed to get them to give me free delivery.

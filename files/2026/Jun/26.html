Friday 26th June

The day started sunny, but during the morning it clouded over, with a strong breeze. It became much more pleasant, but after lunch the cloud cleared. However, it was a slightly more comfortable day.

We walked down to the High Street. I booked an eye test at the optician and then went on to the pharmacy. I'd hoped they would have some Fixadent, but they didn't. Meanwhile Angela went to Morrisons and then to the library.

I wrote three email, one to Evanna Gale at the IET about the lack of torch kits, one to Peter Fussey about a talk to the IET and finally one to Deshinder about the venue for Tom P's talk.

I spent some time listening to a podcast about geothermal energy in Cornwall. I also watched a YouTube video on Copenhagen Atomics thorium reactor.

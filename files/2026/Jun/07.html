Sunday 7th June

While Angela went to church I investigated the Rode microphones some more. Eventually I sent a message to their support organisation and was surprised to get a response later in the day. It seems that they are based in Australia, but gave me details of their UK agent. However, I need proof of purchase. I'd emailed Shanir earlier in the day to see if he has an invoice.

I also found that Rode sell an adapter (basically just a handle) to convert a microphone (transmitter) into a lollypop mic. This is just the sort of thing that could be made using a 3-D printer. So I plan to make a 3-D image of the microphone and ask Austin if he can design and print an adapter.

Later in the afternoon we spoke with Paul and I also updated my MGB articles list, since my last article has been published in the June edition of Main Gear.

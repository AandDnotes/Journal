Thursday 25th June

If anything, it was hotter today than yesterday, which had set a new temperature record for June. We started by going to Lyons Farm to do our weekly shop, partly seeking some air conditioning in supermarkets. We went on to Findon Vale (which has an air conditioned cafe), but noticed drips coming from the front of the car. In case it was engine coolant we decided to come straight home and I phoned Tony at Simms. He said it is likely to be condensation from the aircon unit, but suggested that I take the Panda down there for them to check. So after lunch I took the Panda down there and they put it on a lift. It was just condensation from the aircon. I also told Doug about bleeding the clutch on an MGB.

I spent some more time creating diagrams for my talk on Thorium rectors. Mike had sent a link to a video about Copenhagen Atomic, but it wouldn't run past the first advert.

Tuesday 23rd June

It was too hot to go anywhere, even down to the High Street. The thermometer reached 30 degrees. I cleaned the outside light of cobwebs to see if it will work properly after dark. Angela sewed now tape on to the sail, one of which broke yesterday evening and I took a photo of the new clutch slave cylinder on the B to check that the pipe and bleed nipple had been put in the correct holes. A little later Angela cut the grass.

Then we mainly sat around before I joined the CC-UK meeting at six.

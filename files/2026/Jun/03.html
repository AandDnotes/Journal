Wednesday 3rd June

I was up at six o'clock so as to be at Mike W's by 7:45 and drive off to Maidenbower Junior School to run fanboat workshops. We started by talking to the five, year 6 classes before running two workshops, one in the morning and one in the afternoon. It was a pity that we had some 'horizontal' rain.

Angela went walking with her friends; taking the long way to the Hub and getting very wet on the way home.

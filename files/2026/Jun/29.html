Monday 29th June

Another dry, but cooler day. Angela went to her Monday Club and I ordered some things from Halfords. I'd expected to be able to collect them tomorrow, when Angela wanted to go to M&S, but the leather cleaner/treatment will not be available for collection until 4pm on Thursday. I also arranged to give Mike W a lift to the RCEA committee meeting on Thursday.

Angela pottered in the garden and I popped out to help prune the top of next door's hedge. Otherwise I tried to make sense of my tax documents. Different letters contain different figures! I'm still not sure that one part of HMRC is talking to another part. My latest tax code includes additions to obtain unpaid tax. But another letter asks me to pay money on account that seems to include unpaid tax from previous years.

Later I reviewed the draft RCEA web site and noted some comments.

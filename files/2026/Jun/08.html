Monday 8th June

We went off fairly early to the church to help set up for the lunch club. The main course had been prepared, but Angela and Phyllis had to cook it. I put up the tables and set them. We did not get home until about 3:30.

In the evening I went to the WSMGOC meeting at Washington. We all had to choose our favorite B, GT, F/TF, and others. I had a long journey home, since the road was closed at the Wiston junction.

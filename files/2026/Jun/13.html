Saturday 13th June

A warmer, sunny day. After walking to the High Street to buy a newspaper and some other items I collected together my tools for the afternoon's Repair Cafe and assembled the mower. Then I did the weekly accounts, including a reconciliation against the bank statement.

After lunch Angela cut the grass and I went to the Repair Cafe. I spent the whole time looking at an electric mower. The lady had brought it in last month. It ran for a few seconds and then stopped. We checked continuity and decided that the only thing we couldn't check was the switch. She returned with a new switch, but after dismantling the handle I discovered that the original switch was fine. We concluded that there must be a thermal switch, but couldn't get the motor out to find it. Graham took it home to try to get it apart. The owner was not happy!

Later we spoke to Victoria. At least, as usual, she mainly spoke to us. They have arranged for someone to inspect their pool next week.

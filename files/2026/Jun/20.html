Saturday 20th June

After our walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper I did the weekly accounts. Then I retrieved the money from the Virgin Money account that had matured and opened an ISA for each of us with the Post Office. It took more effort than I thought it should.

After lunch I spent some time looking at the cordless drill that I brought home from the Repair Cafe. I cleaned the battery terminals and squirted some contact cleaner into the trigger, but it still wouldn't start. So I phoned the owner and will take it back to the next Cafe.

Sunday 14th June

While Angela went to church I packaged up the Rode kit, ready to send back for one of the transmitters to be repaired. I made one box and put it inside an existing box. I wrapped the kit in foam and then the inner box in scrunched up magazine pages.

A lot of the rest of the day, working on and off, was spent preparing a video of Tuesday's talk. Finally I uploaded it to the IET Sussex YouTube channel.

During the afternoon we spoke with Paul. Rowan had been trying to prepare for a science test.

Tuesday 30th June

The last day of June. It started raining, dried during the morning and became hot and sunny later.

We started by going to Holebush, where Angela bought some bras in M&S and I bought some trousers in Tesco. We also bought a few food items.

When we got home I received an email (and SMS) from Halfords to say that the leather treatment and metal polish I'd ordered had arrived. I'd expected them to arrive late on Thursday. So I took the B to Shoreham to pick up the items. I stopped at Beeding to fill up with petrol.

Later I tried to remove the headrests in the B, finding that they will not pull right out. So I spent some time finding out why.

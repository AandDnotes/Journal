Saturday 6th June

We dressed up for the rain this morning to go down to the High Street, but it had stopped raining by the time we went out! But it stayed windy all day, with a couple of showers.

As usual, I did the weekly accounts and finished off the share dividends part of the monthly accounts.

After lunch we spoke with Victoria. They'd had a good trip to meet Claudia at a 'wild west' theme park. They were still doing jobs on the house.

After dinner we walked down to the Steyning Centre to see Heber Opera perform shortened versions of Carmen and Turendot. They only had a piano (electric) and it wasn't staged. I was surprised that the only really known 'tune' in Carmen is the Toreador song.

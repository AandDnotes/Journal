Sunday 21st June

The Summer solstice and a hot, sunny one. Since it was so fine I decided to take the B to the SADCASE event at Camelia Botnar. I hadn't planned to stay long, but I bought a coffey and sat with someone from Southwater who had a VW Camper van, although he didn't know about SADCASE. In the end I didn't get home until just before midday.

When I got home, and while Angela was still at church, I updated my 'Work Timeline' page on the B's web site.

Later we spoke to Paul and later still to Victoria, after she and Austin had taken Austin's friend's family to the airport. Earlier she had posted a photo of Juniper and Brandon in their pool.

Thursday 4th June

Another early start, but this time with a traffic jam on the A23 due to an accident near Handcross. So we were a little late getting started in Maidenbow School. However, we had plenty of time and ran workshops for two classes. I was home at about five o'clock.

Angela went to the High Street to do some of our weekly shop. Also, the MGB's new clutch master cylinder arrived.

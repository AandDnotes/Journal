Monday 13th April

A busy start to the week. Angela went off the church to help with the Lunch Club. I joined her just before pwople were expected to arrive. We got home at about three o'clock.

Then I took the B off to Washington for the WSMGOC clun night. I won a raffle prize (a cook book by Jamie Oliver, which will be given to another raffle). Driving home was the first time that I'd driven the B in the dark. The lights are quite good.

When I got home we had a call with Victoria (until bedtime).

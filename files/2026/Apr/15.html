Wednesday 15th April

A drizzely morning saw Angela go off walking with her friends and me drive the B to see Lee on the A26 between Lewes and Uckfield. Angela walked across the White Bridge to the Hub. Lee put the B on a lift and we inspected the sills and underside.

Lee will give me an estimate of the cost to cut out the bottom of the rear wing and weld in new bits. That will include re-painting the repaired area, cleaning the underside and refreshing the underseal. It will also include injecting the sills.

Overall I drove 46 miles, reaching 70 mph a couple of times. The car drove well, although the idle seems a little slow.

After lunch I completed the RCEA's April newsletter. It contains 65 images and the pdf file is 23MB! I sent it to Glyn for review, but I need to find a way of reducing the file side.

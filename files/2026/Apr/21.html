Tuesday 21st April

Before we went out I made an appointment at the dentist to have my tooth replaced and asked Lackfords to send me their estimate again. Then we went to M&S to look at gluten free food in preparation for Paul's visit.

I made the ammendments to the RCEA April newsletter that were sent by the last speaker. Then I sent the revised version to Glyn for review.

Angela went for a walk after lunch and I went to the dentist. She said that the remaining root of my front, right incissor has fractured. She removed a bit and then stuck my crown back in. She thinks it is unlikely to last. Then the options are a denture or an implant. For the latter she will have to remove the root and fill the hole with replacement bone. That will need to be left for six months before a CT scan is made to check that everything is good for an implant. She will then put in a 'narrow' implant and crown. Removing the root and adding 'bone' will cost about £400, the CT scan (done elsewhere) costs £150 and the implant (and crown) is another £1,800!

Thursday 24th April

The day, a fine sunny one, was all about Rowan and Paul arriving. We started by doing the weekly grocery shop, which was a little more than usual.

Their plane arrived a little early and they got through passport control quickly, so they were waiting for us when we arrived. We had to park on the roof!

They spent the remains of the afternoon exploring the house, the gardens and the area. In spite of us setting up the futon for Paul, they both slept in the spare room.

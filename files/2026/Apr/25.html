Saturday 25 April

We all walked down to the High Street to buy a newspaper and things for tomorrow's picnic. We then set off for a walk around the upper horseshoe. We were back in time for a late lunch, but all very tired.

So the afternoon was spent mainly sitting down. We had a quick (surprisingly) call with Victoria. She said that Austin (who was still in bed) would call later to talk to Rowan. But he didn't.

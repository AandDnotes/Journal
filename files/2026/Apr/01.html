Wednesday 1st April

We have heat! And that's not an April Fool. Angela went off walking with her friends and the plumber arrived at 9:45, with lots of copper pipe. He was finished at half past three and the radiators started geting hot. After our experined after Christmas we were surprised how quickly the house heated up.

During the day, sat in front of a fan heater, I wrote an article on the B for Main Gear. An article all about the differences between the TC and the B.

Just before dinner I joined the CC-UK meeting. Two people, one from Foothold and the other from the IET Futures Fund, had been invited to update us about their organisations and see how the CC-UK could help making LNs more aware of what they are doing. Basically they want to raise funds and ensure that LNs/members know what support they offer. Caron, Andrea and I were the only attendees!

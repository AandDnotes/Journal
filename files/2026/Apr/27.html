Monday 27th April

This morning we took Paul and Rowan to Gatwick to catch their flight home. We parked in the short stay car park, which cost £8 for 20 minutes rather than £10 for the 'drop-off'. Their flight was on time and the got home safely.

On our way home we called in to Lackfords. The man had not re-sent the estimate, which was still at home! But he said that the cost was estimated to be £3,000 to £3,500 all in (assuming that they don't find any more problems). So later I asked Lee if he could do it while we were in the Netherlands in July.

When I checked the Eurostar timetable it said that no trains were available for four days over the time we had planned our outbound journey. So I booked tickets for Monday 20th (which is actually the day that Victoria et al arrive), with a return on Tuesday 28th. I booked 'club' class, so I can change the tickets at any time. Using the vouchers from our Christmas trip I didn't have to pay anything. In fact I have £35 still to use. I then completed the 'advanced passenger information' and downloaded the tickets.

Later I ordered two new bulbs for the oven.

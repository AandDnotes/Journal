Sunday 26th April

Another fine day, which was good, because it was 'Drive It Day'. We set off at ten, in both cars, for Sheffield Park station on the Bluebell Railway. There were a lot of MGs from the WSMGOC, and a lot of volunteer marshals. Rowan and I (in the B) parked with the MGs, but Angela and Paul (in the Panda) had to park in the main car park.

We caught the train to Kingscote station, where we had our picnic lunch. Afterwards we caught the train on to East Grinstead, where we bought icecream, looked in the carriage workshop and then caught the same train back to Sheffied Park.

We had a quick look in the engine shed before driving home. Rowan came in the B on both journeys. We did a total of 54 miles, with the hood down.

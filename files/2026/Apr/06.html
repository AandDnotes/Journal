Monday 6th April

Easter Monday, and a clear blue sky. Rather than Angela's Monday club, she joined the usual participants at Phylis's. I did some work on the RCEA April newsletter, having received George's 'Presidents message'. I also added some links to the newsletter index that I had started to compile.

Then I went into the garage to check over the cars; tyres, oil and water. I also examined one of the air filters, which was clean, but discovered that its attachment to the carburettor had a bracket that holds a cable (the choke, I think). I also found that the rubber pipe that joins the air filters was very old and very loose. Luckily I can get one from the MGOC spares site. It took some effort to get it all back together.

Angela suggested that we went for a walk, so after lunch I put down the hood and we took the B to Shoreham (so as to test that I'd put it back together properly) and then walked past the house boats, over the foot bridge, back along the High Street and over the Norfolk Bridge. The walk was gentle and the car ran well.

We got home just in time to have a catch-up with Paul. A little later Victoria messaged to say that they have booked their flights to the Netherlands in July/August. We can now plan our trip.

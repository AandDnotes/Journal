Wednesday 8th April

Sunshine from dawn to dusk; and the hottest day of the year so far. In fact, our outside thermometer reached twenty degrees.

The day was really about the Sussex Wanderers run. We took the B, with the hood down, to Ashington to join up with the others and have coffee. We then drove along familiar roads to Shoreham for lunch. We did a total of 36 miles.

I had hoped to take the B to have the rot in the sills looked at on Friday, but they don't have a lift free that day, so it will need to be next week.

I did a little work on the RCEA newsletter index, sending George W pdf and HTML versions.

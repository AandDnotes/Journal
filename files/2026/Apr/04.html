Saturday 4th April

A busier day than we had planned, at least during the morning. We started by walking to the High Street and whilst there we met Anne and Harold who reminded us about the Museum coffee morning. So after visiting the butchers and the Sussex Grocer we went to the Penfold Hall.

Apart from coffee and buscuits, we won a raffle prize. I chose a box of chocolates. We then got home just before I had to go out again. I went to Southlands Hospital to have my pelvis x-ray'd. My appointent was at one o'clock, but I got there at 12:30. I'd had my x-rays and was out by ten to one.

We normally speak to Victoria after lunch, but they had Holly and Kyle with them (for a long weekend), to work on the house. So I spent a lot of the afternoon reading the newspaper.

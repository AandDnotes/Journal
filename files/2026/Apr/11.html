Saturday 11th April

A grey day became brighter as it went on, but it was cool and breezy. We started with a walk to the High Street to by a newspaper and a couple of other things. Then I did the weekly accounts, before creating the documents for Tueseday's IET talk.

After lunch it was the Repair Cafe.I didn't do very well. I spent the whole time looking at a Kitchenmaid toaster that was not showing any signs of life. It took a while to get the top off, after which I had to put back the screws that I'd removed that held parts to the base. The element seemed fine and the electrolytic capacitors showed no sign of damage, but that was all I could check. It had a motor (to lower and raise the slices of bread) and lots of electronics. But all the wire connections had shrink-wrap over them and I couldn't test anything. So I brought home a Bose music system that will not play CDs. It is unlikely that I will be able to fix it, but it shows willing.

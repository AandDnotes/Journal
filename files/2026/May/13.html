Wednesday 13th May

A day of sunshine and showers. The early morning started with a clear blue sky. Then there wer heavy showers, with hailstones and spells of sunshine.

Angela was lucky and stayed dry on her walk in Shoreham with her friends. I decided not to go into the garage, but investigated the recordings from yesterday evening's talk.

The first part of the talk used only the camera's built-in microphone, but then changed to the Rode microphone. Only one of the Rode transmitters worked, so I could retrieve the audio recordings. Unfortunately it looked as though the faulty transmitter produced a few loud, high-pitched sounds, although only for a few seconds. So I added parts of the sound track to the video.

During the afternoon I went to the dentist (in the hail) to have my denture fitted. THe dentist put it in, but then could't get it out!. Eventually it came out and she ground part of the plate. I have to keep the denture in overnight and return tomorrow afternoon. The first meal was difficult.

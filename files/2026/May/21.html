Thursday 21st May

Shopping at Lidl and Sainsbury's was followed by coffee at Findon Vale. It was warmer than it looked.

When we got home I found the delivery from the MGOC. So I took them down to Simms and spoke with Doug. He had to lowere the gearbox a bit in order to fill it with oil. He also thinks that the brake light problem is due to a faulty switch. So when I got home I ordered a new one.

After lunch Angela washed the Panda, which had been covered by a gull, and then then cleared weeds from the drive. I swept the garage floor and pulled up and burnt weeds along the edge of the grage door. We also put out the water feature.

Later I bought a ticket for a talk tomorrow evening that is part of the Steyning Festival.

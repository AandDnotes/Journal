Saturday 2nd May

Angela was up early making a chocolate roulade for today's lunches at the Flower Festival. I went down shortly after to help carry Phylis's cakes around to the church and then went to buy a newspaper. Angela continued making lunches and I sold lunch tickets. We eventually had our lunch at two o'clock and were home just before four.

The weather was brighter and warmer than expected, but just after I got home it started raining heavily. Angela got very wet.

Later I added the fitting of a new connector to the B's air filters to my 'Small jobs' web page.

Sunday 3rd May

Quite a spendid day. I took the B down to the High Street for the Steyning Vintage Revival. There were already many classic cars in plkace and more arrive after me.

Angela came down to look around and have coffee, before we walked home for lunch. Angela then went down to the church to help serve teas and later I walked back down to the B. There were a lot of people showing interest in the cars.

We could move the cars from 4 o'clock and I came home. Angela followed a while later. After putting the dinner in the oven we spoke with Paul, seeing their puppy, now named Hilda!

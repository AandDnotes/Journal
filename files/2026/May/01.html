Friday 1st May

A mainly sunny and warm day with light winds, but perhaps the last for a while. So I took the B over to Mike W's to cut holes in pieces of foam that form the body of the LED torches. Mike was repairing fan boat motors/battery boxes. I spent nearly three hours there.

During the afternoon I fitted the new rubber 'hose' that joins the air filters. There were a lot of bits to hold together so as to refit the rear air filter.

Tuesday 5th May

Quite a tiring day for both of us. Angela went to the church to help clean up and put things away. Then she baked cakes during the afternoon and cooked a batch of minced beef, one of which became a cottage pie for dinner.

I spent a good deal of time trying to change the antifreeze in the B. It took a lot of effort to get the radiator bottom hose off. I then filled it with rain water, ran the engine and, after more struggle to get the hose off, drained it again. However, I'd forgotten that there is a drain plug on the block. Trying to undo it proved problematic, since the distributor and the steering column are in the way. So I left it until tomorrow.

My original plan was to change the antifreeze in the morning and try to put the car up on axle stands. While waiting for the rubber hose to 'relax' I made a wooden interface between the trolley jack and the front cross member.

During the afternoon, while in the garage, I had a call from Alan. We will see him and Chris at Martin's funeral.

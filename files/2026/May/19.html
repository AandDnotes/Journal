Tuesday 19th May

We drove over 130 miles today (according to Google Maps). We went to my cousin Martin's funeral near Maidstone, so a lot of motorway and a lot of rain on the journey there. It was interesting to hear more about his life, even though I had little interaction with him when he was alive. Apart from Ros, the only people we knew were Alan and Chris and Anthony and Jill.

We left home at 9:30, arriving at the crematorium at 11:30, which included a stop for coffee. Our journey home was quicker. We left just before three and were home before 4:30, feeling quite tired.

When we got home (and after a cup of tea) I checked the B's windscreen washer and lights. The driver's side washer doesn't work and the brake lights seem too weak. I checked the nearside rear bulb (which was fine) and poked the nozzels with a pin. But to remove the nozzle looks 'impossible'. So I added both to my list of jobs to be done by Doug at Simms.

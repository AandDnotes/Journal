Saturday 9th May

We started with a walk to the High Street. In addition to the usual newspaper, Angela had to get some things for Monday's lunchclub and I had to collect my tablets from the pharmacy.

I put my collection of tools together for the afternoon and did the weekly accounts before lunch. Then it was off to the Repair Cafe. It was fairly quiet the five of us on teh electrical bench were fairly busy, even though two of us sometimes worked worked on the same item.

The temperature, as indicated on our outside thermometer, moved above twenty degrees.

Wednesday 6th May

Angela went off walking with her friends and I went back into the garage. I failed to budge the drain plug on the block, even after getting out a box spanner and a collection of bit. I removed the distributer cap and rotor arm, but still no joy.

So I ran the engine for a while in the hope that the pump would force moor coolant out of the bottom hose. Then, after a struggle to get the front air filter assembly back, I discovered that my antifreeze container had water in it! I had kept it in the TC's boot. So I went to Henfield to get some ethylene glycol based antifreeze concentrate.

I put four litres of water (filtered from the water butt) and one litre of antifreeze in the radiator, which filled it. I ran the engine for a short while, but the level didn't go down, although it wasn't long enough to open the thermostat. I will have to take the car for a run.

It took a while to put everything back in the garage.

Tuesday 12th May

Angela walked to the High Street three times, because she forgot to buy something each of the first two times. I spent a lot of time in the garage.

I lifted the front of the car using the trolley jack, but couldn't see anywhere suitable to put axle stands. I lowered it to the ground and then lifted the back using the differential. I put axle stands (the adjustable ones on their lowest position) under the front of the springs.

Even though the back axle lowered, at this height it was difficult to reach the bolts on the rear cover plate on the differential. I put some spray on the rusty exhaust U-clamp, but I couldn't move the nuts.

So I made a list of jobs to ask Simms to do. It is getting longer. I've decided not to try putting the B up on four stands. I will raise either the front or the back as required, although I can probably do all the service items (that I can do) by just lifting one wheel at a time off the ground.

Later in the day I drove to Crawley to the IET talk. It was good and quite well attended. However, I'm not sure that the microphones have recorded everything. I'll find out tomorrow.

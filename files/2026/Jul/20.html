Monday 20th July

A long and tiring day. We got up early and caught the 9:30 bus to Shoreham. We were early enough to catch the earlier train heading for London Victoria. So we took that to Burgess Hill and waited the eight minutes for the ThamesLink train to St Pancras. So we bought a few things (forgetting to get an extra power adapter) and then checked in to the Eurostar to Rotterdam. It was a comfortable journey which included a light meal.

We arrived in Rotterdam at the platform next to the train to Gouda, which was standing there. We boarded the train, then realised that we hadn't used our UV-chip cards. So we got off and tapped the cards on the only reader we could see. However, when we got to Gouda the system took €20 off each of our cards. A Netherlands Railway man tried to help, but didn't know what had happened. He said we should contact customer support tomorrow to see if we can get it put right.

Paul picked us up and we were in Berkenwoude by about nine o'clock. We were met by Austin, Rowan, Juniper and, of course, Hilda! Victoria was also still up, but Brandon had gone to bed.

Sunday 12th July

Another hot, but very windy day. Angela went off to church and stayed on for a farewell lunch for the Minister. I unpacked my tool bag in the garage and then smoothed the paint that I'd applied to the Panda's rear bumper, with some 'wet & dry'. The paint is not quite the right colour, but it's better than black marks.

Before Angela got back I looked further at river cruises on the Danube, estimating the total cost, including parking and insurance. Later I showed what I'd found to Angela about a cruise in October this year with Riviera Travel. Since there were only two cabins left I transferred money to our current account (to also cover my required payments to HMRC) and tried to book the trip. The final step said something like "Authorising payment. Do not refresh the screen." Unfortunately it stuck there! Of course, their phone number is not manned until tomorrow morning.

A little earlier we spoke with Paul.

Wednesday 15th July

While Angela went off walking with her friends I did a number of small jobs. The walker's plan was thwarted when the route the bus should have taken was blocked in Bramber. So they got off and walked to the Hub!

I walked to the High Street to get orange juice and cheese and then emailed Lee about taking the B to him on Friday, also asking him about photos, paint and bleeding the clutch. He replied later in the positive. Then I looked up times of trains and busses for our trip to the Netherlands on Monday. I also started looking at how we are going to get to Gatwick in October for our Danube cruise. We decided that it would be wise to stay at Gatwick the night before.

At six I joined the Anglian Coast committee meeting, which went on until about ten to eight. They had brought the meeting forward an hour so that they could watch the World Cup semi-final, England v Argentina.

Wednesday 22nd July

A good, but tiring day. We (all except Krista) went off fairly early to the Bird Zoo (actually called Avfauna). It was about 45 minutes drive and unfortunately was drizzling when we got there. It then rained more heavily for a while. However, the zoo was very good, particularly the bird shows (there were two). They let swarms of exotic birds fly freely around and had to be seen to be believed.

We stopped at the pet shop and at Lidl on the way home. By the time we got home we were all very tired and so just had battered fish and chips for dinner, followed by ice cream.

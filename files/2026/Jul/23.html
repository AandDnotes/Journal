Thursday 23rd July

A quieter day. Apart from some people taking Hilda for a walk, we didn't go out. There was a shower of rain when we got up (at about eight), but was otherwise overcast with a sunny evening.

Paul started repairing the render around the base of the join between the cottage and the barn (I advised) and later, while Hilda was out cleaned out the guinea pig cage.

Otherwise we mainly sat around, either inside or out. Some time was spent debating what we should do and where we should go tomorrow.

Wednesday 1st July

Being a Wednesday, Angela went off this morning with her walking friends. I spent most of the morning cleaning the B, just using a 'not too wet' cloth and water containing a squirt of washing-up liquid. I then dried it with a microfibre cloth. Later I made a tool to help remove the headlight surrounds and cleaned the chrome forward of the tonneau area (apart from the wheels that is). The seam on one of the headlight surrounds has started to come apart. At some time I might need to silver solder it. I also managed to remove (and replace) the headrests, simply by giving them a sharp jerk upwards. I had made a 'tool' to slide down the edge of the 'pole' in the hope that it would help release the spring clip, but it turned out to be too short.

After lunch the 'chair man' arrived. He had been sent to Penlands Rise and so had met a surprised resident. It seems that the noise made by my chair was simply the zip tags catching on the reclining mechanism! So all is now well.

I booked two things and Angela booked one. I bought our train tickets to get to St Pancras and back, and then booked an appointment for a hair cut (on Friday). Angela booked an eye test in the High Street.

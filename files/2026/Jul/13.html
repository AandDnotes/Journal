Monday 13th July

I started by phoning Riviera Travel to find out if my attempt at booking a river cruise had worked. I found that it had, and later an acknowledgement arrived after they had checked that the flights were available. Angela went off early to help set up the Lunch Club and I joined her nearer lunch time.

Later I checked our finances and paid my tax bill. I also obtained a quote for travel insurance and bought that. I now just have airport parking to arrange and passport information to add. I also printed our train and Eurostar tickets.

Then Angela checked that Christina is Felicity's middle name. So I submitted my LPA, paying for it in the process.

Later still I took the B to Washington for a meeting of the WSMGOC. There were 32 MGs there. I was home at nine.

Thursday 2nd July

Mike W arrived at ten past nine and I drove to Rustington for our RCEA committee meeting. We gathered comments and views on the proposed new web site. We were back by about 12:15.

I finished cleaning the chrome on the B, then vacuumed the inside. I also investigated the rattling door mirrors. I removed the mirror (the driver's side had a dried-up piece of rubber seal inside) and cut a one inch length of radiator hose, the diameter of which was just right to fit behind the mirror and stop it rattling.

Friday 20th March

It seems that today was the Spring Equinox, not Wednesday. However, it was sunny from start to finish, even if there was at times a little high-level cloud.

We took the B out for a run to Highdown Gardens, a round trip of 29 miles. The hood was down, of course, and I used the tonneau to cover the interior while we left the car.

When we arrived home the drive belt for the cine projector had arrived. So after lunch I fitted it and reassembled the machine. To my surprise the projector then worked. So later I spoke to the owner to arrange its return.

I created additions to my 'outings' and 'small jobs' web pages for the MGB.

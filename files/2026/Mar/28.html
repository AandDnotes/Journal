Saturday 28th March

My first job today was to phone Jo Willett to accept his estimate for running a new gas pipe around thre house outside. I left a message and he called back later. So I did two bank transfers, one to pay his bill for yeasterday and the other as a deposit for the work. He wanted 70%, which I think is a bit much, but I paid it. He will do the work on Wednesday.

While this was happening, Angela went to the High Street, twice, to get a newspaper and other things. It was sunny, but breezy and chilly. Later it changed to sunny intervals and showers (and hail storms).

After lunch we spoke with Victoria. The grandchildren have been on their spring break, but don't seem to have done anything. Although Juniper spent one night in a tent in the garden with two friends.

Later I sent the plumber's estimate to Octopus.

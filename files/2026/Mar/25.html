Wednesday 25th March

The day started as usual for a Wednesday. The rain stopped just before Angela went off walking with her friends. I produced a lteer to HMRC about my savings interest for 2024/25 and put all the pieces of paper in an envelope. Angela posted it later. Then I started processing the photos that Mike W sent me for the report on his houseboat talk.

At about 12:30 the Octopus engineer arrived to fit new smart meters to our electricity and gas supplies. Fitting the new electricity meter went well. However, after fitting the device on the gas meter, tests for gas leaks failed. THe leak was diagnosed as being in the iron pipe the goes from the meter, within the concrete floor, to our boiler and hob.

Consequently the gas supply had to be capped and so we have no heating or hot water (although we do have an immersion heater in the tank). I arranged for a gas fitter to call on Friday to give us an estimate for fixing it, which will probably be via a new pipe running around the house! We at least have the oven and microwave for cooking.

Saturday 14th March

We woke to a clear blue sky and it stayed sunny all day. Our usual walk to the High Street was, this morning, to buy a little more than a newspaper. At least it weighed a lot more!

When we got home I put my tool bag together for the afternoon and then did the weekly accounts, including a reconcilliation against the bank statement. I have to sort out a direct debit, that is marked as being 'new', but is only described as 'charity'. I also have to note some dividend payments and a maturing ISA account.

After lunch it was the Repair Cafe. We only had four people on our bench and a constant stream of people. In fact some needed to be turned away. I didn't bring anything home, although I will see if I can look at an 8mm cine projector that needs a new drive belt. It wouldn't transport the film since the original belt had gone to 'goo'. It will take more time clearing up the mess than fitting a new belt.

Angela spent a lot of the day working in the garden, as well as doing two lots of washing.

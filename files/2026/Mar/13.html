Friday 13th March

It rained heavily overnight, with a strong wind, but was dry during the day. I was up early so as to get to SeTyres at Henfield to have tyres fitted to the B. I was there just before they opened at 8:30 and the car was ready just after ten. I walked up and down the High Street and then went into a cafe for a coffee. Then I sat in the SeTyres office, reading my magazine.

When I got home I put the covers back in the boot and then found that they had inflated the new tyres to about 35 psi. I normally use 24 psi at the front and 27 psi at the rear, so since the tyres were still a little warm I set them to 26/29.

After lunch I did more Dutch lessons than usual, so as to get a little ahead. Otherwise I mainly read the paper and made a few notes about the B.

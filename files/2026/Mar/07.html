Saturday 7th March

We took our usual walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper and some meat for tomorrow. Then I updated the IET Sussex web page about last Tuesday's talk, before doing the weekly accounts.

After lunch we set off in the direction of Chichester. Firstly we dropped off David F-B's music centre. He had transferred £35 into one of our accounts, so I can make a £15 donation to the Repair Cafe. Then we went on to the Avisford Park Hotel for the WSMGOC Annual Dinner. This is the only event in our calendar for which I can wear my dinner jacket and bow tie.

The hotel room was fine, although could do with an update. The bar, where we met before the dinner, was too noisy to talk, but the dining room was good and we had a good meal (for 60 people), even if the delay between courses was a bit long. The starters were interesting, the main course (we both had a lamb steak) was good and the deserts were not quite as expected from the name. Overall it was a good evening and we finally got to our room at about 10:30.

Tuesday 3rd March

An early start and a late finish. I was up at 6:30 so as to get to Henfield by 8:30. However, when I got there I found that only three of the five tyres had been delivered. So I came straight home. I will try agin tomorrow afternoon.

This evening I gave a talk to the IET Sussex Network in Crawley. So I didn't get home until about 9:30. We only had 18 people there, including me! But the talk seemed to go well. I had run though the slides earlier in the day.

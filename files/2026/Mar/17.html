Tuesday 17th March

A fine day, that turned sunny quite early. It was also quite a busy day. Angela walked down to the High Street twice. I started by downloading some missing Octopus Energy statements. Then I went into the garage. I gave the pedals cover another coat of black paint and then painted the 'rusty' area around the pedal box with dark green Hammerite. It is darker than the car's green, but I can always cover the area with the right colour when I get some. Hopefully I'll be able to put it back together tomorrow.

After lunch I started work on the cine projector. It took quite a long time (plus a lot of kitchen towel and surgical spirit) to remove all the black 'goo' that had once been the drive belt. A new belt should arrive in the next day or two.

Before dinner I attended an IET online meeting about the IET's proposed new governence structure. The Board and Council will dissapear, leaving just the Trustees, but the 'Operational Committees' (of which the CC-UK is one) are yet to be reviewed. So we didn't learn anything about the future of the CC-UK.

Sunday 15th March

While Angela went to church (taking the laminator with her) I set the maturity option for one of Angela's ISAs and noted some dividends. Then I went into the garage to look at the rust around the pedal box under the bonnet. I removed the cover and scraped away the paint and rust. I used the old vacuum cleaner to pick up the pieces.

After lunch we spoke to both Victoria and Paul. In April, Rowan has a two-week holiday and she and Paul plan to come over to visit us for a few days.

Wednesday 18th March

The Spring Equinox. And another busy day. Angela went walking with her friends, starting at the old station at West Grinstead and walking up to the Soutwater Country Park on the Downs Link. They stopped for coffee and then walked back.

I received confirmation that Jeremy H could give a talk on flight simulators in April to the IET. So I spent some time sending details to Dave P and updating documents on our IET Sussex web page. I also added a note about the talk on the page and sent the details to Mark C for posing on our socials.

Then it was into the garage. I put the pedal box cover back, fixing the accelerator cable to the top. I also checked the tyre pressures, the engine oil and radiator water before removing each plug. They were all a nice straw colour.

After lunch I found a video about the cine projector and removed a cover that gave me easier access to the belt pulley. That allowed me to remove the rest of the 'goo'.

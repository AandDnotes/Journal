Friday 5th April

Today was our Thursday in so far as we did our main grocery shop today. However, we started by taking a collection of things to the Greenhouse charity shop in Worthing. Unfortunately they did not want any more books and we had quite a lot, but they did take the rest, including some Camping-gaz canisters. We then went on to park in Waitrose, walked to B&M to buy some bird food and to Dyas for some paper napkins for the lunch club before returning to Waitrose for coffee and to do our shop.

Since workmen were about to start spreading stones on the Bostal Road, we returned via Findon Vale garden centre to get some orchid pots and compost.

Later I prepared as much of the next Adestra email as possible and updated the programme of events document.

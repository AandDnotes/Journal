Friday 19th April

Then we set off to the Old Barn garden centre, stopping at Squires to buy some asparagus (and one or two other things). We had coffee and Angela's piece of 'free' cake. Then we bought birthday cards for Rowan and Austin. When we got home (in time to make lunch) we found a package on the doorstep. Kate had warned Angela to expect a parcel today and it turned out to be a Cornish Cream Tea for two. We eat them for our pudding in the evening.

A bunch of flowers was delivered just after lunch and later we also had a surprise, short FaceTime call from Rowan and Paul.

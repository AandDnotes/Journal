Sunday 14th April

Not so warm today and it remained overcast, although dry. Angela went off to church and I produced the start slide for Tuesday's talk. I also made a speaker certificate, only to find that the printer had run out of the three coloured inks. It took me quite a while to get the certificate printed. I also charged the camera and microphone batteries and looked at the new microphones.

After lunch we spoke firstly to Victoria and then to Paul. As well as looking after Alfred, the tortoise, they have a duck nesting on the edge of their drive!

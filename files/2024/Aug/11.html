Sunday 11th August

A quieter day. Some went off early to take Austin to a medical centre in Goude. They were back early, with Austin's bandaged arm in a sling and instructions to phone the X-ray department early in the morning. Victoria then phoned their insurance company about the medical treatment.

It was a hot sunny day. Paul, Angela and Brandon took Flo for a walk, then Brandon worked on his job application and I did some more to my fission talk.

Later we had a barbecue and then played swing-ball. Later still, we watched the closing ceremony of the Olympic Games, until the speeches started.

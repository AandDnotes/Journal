Saturday 10th August

Another interesting day. We had a quiet morning, but after lunch we (Paul and I) cycled to the playground on the outskirts of Berkenwoude. Paul took the children in the box-bike and the others walked there. I was quite 'wobbly'. Just as the others arrived so Austin fell off the see-saw and hurt his wrist. We brought them home, gave him paracetamol and put his arm in a sling. Later, Krista phoned the hospital and they suggested taking him there in the morning.

Victoria emailed their travel insurer about their delayed departure and the extra cost of their hire car. She then phoned them about Austin.

Monday 19th March

The post that arrived this morning included a Halifax letter for Krista. We were expecting just a code, but it was a device into which she needs to enter the code so as to generate another code to sign in to online banking. So I took it to the Post Office and sent it to Krista.

We ate at lunchtime, since I went to an IET talk in the evening. I downloaded the attendance list and printed it.

At the talk I welcomed people and then handed over to Daniel of the ADSC to introduce the speaker. I wasn't very impressed with the talk, just a few examples of collaborative robots and a few philosophical questions.

The journey home was more difficult, since the A27 was closed.

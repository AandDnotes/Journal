Wednesday 20th March

I think the only time I stepped outside today was to open the back gate for the window cleaner. It was a gloriously sunny and calm afternoon and I had the patio door wide open, so I had to quickly shut it and the other windows.

I spent a lot of the day downloading and manipulating video files from yesterday's talk. By dinner time I had a completed recording, ready to be uploaded to YouTube.

As usual on a Wednesday, Angela went to table tennis, although this was her last session until the autumn. Next week she will return to going out with the walking group.

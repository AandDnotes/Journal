Saturday 14th December

Still chilly, grey and calm, but dry. So we made our usual walk to the High Street to buy an newspaper. Then Angela hung out two batches of washing while I did the weekly accounts. I also opened a new account, moved some money into it and set up a regular, monthly transfer.

We were hoping to talk with Victoria after lunch but they were busy packing, so we spoke with Paul instead. They had all been unwell, but are now better. Later I wrote out the Christmas cracker jokes and cut them into strips. I will try rolling them up.

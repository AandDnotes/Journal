Thursday 23rd May

A complete change in the weather. It was warm(ish), dry and mainly sunny. In fact, we didn't need a coat, just a thin sweater. Although our main aim was to do the weekly grocery shop, we started by going to Squires garden centre, primarily to get some compost, using a voucher. Angela also bought a plant, a packet of seeds and a present for Kate.

We then went into Worthing to Lidl and then Waitrose, before going into the centre to take an ISA maturity form to Nationwide and to buy a few things. We were later than usual home and so had a slightly late lunch.

During the afternoon I started washing the garage door. I did the edges and the joints between the horizontal sections and then washed half of the panels. Hopefully I will get the remainder done tomorrow.

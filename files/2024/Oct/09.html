Wednesday 9th October

Another quite busy day, and a dry one. Angela's Wednesday walk turned into walking around the streets of Steyning, delivering envelopes, before stopping for coffee. I drove to the Spur at Slindon to meet up with David F-B and the rest of the Sussex Wanderers. David was in his MGF.

We had a good run, with the roof down. The curry was also good and I got home at about three o'clock.

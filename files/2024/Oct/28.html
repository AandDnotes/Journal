Monday 28th October

There was no sign of the sun today. In fact it started raining just before lunch and continued for the rest of the day and evening.

While Angela went to her Monday club, I went into the garage and tried using a cloth, polishing wheel on the TC's wing. It made no difference. However, later in the afternoon I had a call from Tony at Milestone Classics to say that they had received an enquiry. He wanted to know if I wanted them to pursue it. I said yes and he asked if Jamie could come over to take a 'full circle' video of the car with the engine running. We settled on Friday, which is forecast to be dry.

I 'waisted' the rest of the morning completing the Rubrik's cube, following an online set of algorithms. It took a while to find where I was going wrong, but I finished it eventually. Later I mixed it up started trying to solve it without (too much) reference to the web site.

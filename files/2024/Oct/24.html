Thursday 24th October

Supermarket shopping day. We started by going to Lidl at Lyons Farm and then over to Sainsbury's. I also took our new front door key back to Timpsons and they cut us a new one, since I had to put tape on the key to stop it going in too far. Then we went off to Findon Vale garden centre for coffee.

It was sunny and calm for most of the day, so after lunch Angela did some work in the garden and I went into the garage. I did a little experiment, rubbing down a patch of the offside front wing where their were 'pimples'. It seemed to improve it, so I did the pimply areas of both front wings. I started with emery paper, lubricated with water with a little washing up liquid in it. Then I used some T-cut, before applying some wax. The wings look better, although not as shiny.

Later I looked at some UK-CC documents in preparation for tomorrow's meeting. I'm not sure they helped a lot!

Saturday 19th October

Angela was collected (in the rain) quite early and taken off to Eastbourne for the day. I made two trips to the High Street to buy a newspaper. The papers had not arrive (anywhere) when I got there the first time. Luckily the rain had stopped before the first walk and the sun was shining for the second.

For me it was then the weekly accounts, followed by moving some money from our current account to a savings account. Then reading the paper and doing my Dutch lessons before Angela arrived home just before four o'clock.

We then had a chat with Victoria. Juniper had been ill with a fever all week.

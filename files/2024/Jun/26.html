Wednesday 26th June

An even hotter day. Angela went off walking with her friends in the morning, going around the lower horseshoe (again) because it is shaded by trees. I put my overalls on over my pants and continued working in the garage. I:

  • Gave both sides of the three patches another coat of Hammerite.
  • Put a coat of Hammerite on the base of the side screens box.
  • Checked the oil level in the rear dampers, topping up the off-side damper.
  • Topped up the oil in the differential.
  • Removed the rear off-side brake drum and cleaned it out. There seems to be a very slight weep from the slave cylinder and there was quite a lot of dust.
  • Removed the spare wheel, gave it a clean and put a spot of white Hammerite over a spot on the 'knock-off' where the chrome had flaked off.
  • Cleaned under the rear wheel arches.
  • Cleaned the chassis from the rear to the front of the springs.

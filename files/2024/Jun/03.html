Monday 3rd June

This morning was pretty much normal for a Monday. Angela went to her Monday club and I finished the monthly accounts and finished winding my magnetic actuator coil. I had to unwind it quite a bit and one side of the cardboard spool has come unglued, so I have some fixing to do. Unusually, I also did my Dutch, since we would be out after lunch.

After an early lunch we set off to Sayers Common to SpaMedica. Today was my appointment for my second cataract surgery. I went in almost immediately and we were out in just over two hours. The surgeon was quite brusk and rough. He seemed as though he was in a hurry. The rest of the staff were very good.

When we got home Angela cut the lawns. My left eye ached and watered much more than the right eye did.

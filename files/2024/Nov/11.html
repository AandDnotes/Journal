Monday 11th November

Quite a busy day and a little brighter than past days. Tony at Milestones phoned to say that the person who viewed the TC was unhappy about a 'repair to the tub', which there isn't. I can only assume that he was looking at the patches on the bottom of the side-screen box that I fitted in June. I sent an image of the panels to Tony so that he could explain to the person that he was wrong.

Angela went off to the lunch club, later than usual since she was only washing up. I went later. Before we started eating I had a call from someone in Worthing who would like to view the TC! I spoke to him later and arranged that he would call in the morning.

Angela then went to a table tennis session, getting back just before I went to the WSMGOC meeting.

Friday 5th January

It wasn't raining when we got up! After breakfast I booked an appointment at the barbers in the High Street for later in the morning.

Angela started preparing to cook various things and I walked down for my haircut, done by a lady from Austria. She cut it shorter than I wanted, but Angela liked it.

Immediately after lunch we went to Dunelm (again) and ordered some curtains for the dining room. The traffic was heavy and we got caught twice at the level crossing.

Before lunch I had started responding to the CAA consultation, as urged by both the BMFA and the SRFC. I'd not realised that there were 35 questions, so it took a long time, even when copying and pasting the comments from the SRFC suggested responses. I saved my entry part way through and finished it later. I also glued the final pieces of foam along the top of the tiny Tiger Moth fuselage and added the rear skid.

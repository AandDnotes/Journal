Wednesday 24th January

It was bright this morning after overnight rain. However, it soon dried up. I started by drafting the next IET email about our next talks. Then we finally got around to pruning the espalier apple tree, since all the leaves had fallen off.

After lunch Angela went to table tennis and I walked down to the Steyning Centre with her. I went on through the churchyard, along Tanyard Lane, up to Charlton Street and then home, avoiding mud since I had earlier cleaned the soles of my walking shoes and boots.

When I got home I corrected errors in my IET email and submitted it for approval.

Wednesday 17th January

Another cold, dry, but grey day. Luckily we didn't get any snow, which was a possibility. It was also a reasonably successful day.

I started by testing the tiny TM with the transmitter. All seemed to be well, so I discharged the battery to storage level and hung up the plane. Then I searched for details of how to dismantle the hand mixer and found a useful video on YouTube. So, after checking the fuse in the plug, I started taking apart the mixer from the Repair Cafe. It was very difficult, but during the afternoon I got it all apart. On the way I checked the mains cord and the EMI filter. However, the PCB was coated, so I couldn't even check the thermal fuse. The ribbon cable joining the controls to the main board was loose, but that may have happened during dismantling.

Angela sorted through more 'family' photos, shredded old documents and went to table tennis during the afternoon.

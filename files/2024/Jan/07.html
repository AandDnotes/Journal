Sunday 7th January

Another dry, but not very productive day. Angela went to church and I did a few jobs, together with a little more work on the tiny Tiger Moth.

Work on the model continued later in the afternoon. I finished fitting all the fuselage 'panels' and put on the 'stickers' to add colour and design to the top, front and part of the bottom. I also put the wheels together.

While we were having dinner Paul called. They had been to Goude to go ice skating (posting some photos) and had been to the pancake house for dinner.

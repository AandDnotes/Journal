Monday 29th January

Quite a busy day. I collected Peter W at nine o'clock and went to Shoreham station, arriving early since the road works had gone. However, we caught the 9:43 train to Victoria, meeting up with the others when we arrived. The pub in which we normally had coffee was closed, since a bus had run into a bus shelter and the whole area was cordoned off.

We walked to the London Transport Museum, via Savoy Place. We started with some lunch in their cafe and then went into the museum. It was quite interesting, but I can't see a reason to go there again during the period of the annual ticket. We all met in the cafe again for a cup of tea, before setting off for the usual pub for dinner. The food was worse than usual, so we won't go there again.

We then caught the 7:16 train home. I got home a little before nine.

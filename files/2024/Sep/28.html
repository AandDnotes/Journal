Saturday 28th September

A sunny, but chilly start to the day. In fact it stayed sunny all day. As usual we started by walking to the High Street to buy a newspaper. It is a pity that it contained the magazine from a different paper.

Also as usual on a Saturday morning I did the weekly accounts. The I examined the IET's video camera and microphones, charging the batteries, deleting the old audio files and testing one of the microphones.

After lunch we spoke with Victoria. They were preparing for Junipers birthday party (her birthday was on Monday). They were expecting nine girls, some of them staying over night. A lot of the rest of the day was spent reading the paper.

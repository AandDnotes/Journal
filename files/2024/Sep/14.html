Saturday 14th September

Another sunny, but initially chilly day. We made our usual trip to the High Street to buy a newspaper (and a few other things). Then it was a case of updating the accounts and reconciling then against the bank statement, which covered our period in the Netherlands.

After lunch it was time to go to the Repair Cafe. There were only three of us on the electrical bench, so we were busy throughout and I brought a kettle and a bread maker home to look at.

We spent the evening watching the Last Night of the Proms.

Tuesday 25th January

I had a short word with Dad (we were cut off, or perhaps Dad inadvertently pressed a button) and then I booked a visit. We decided to have an early lunch and then go shopping in Worthing.

We parked on the front, just passed Heene Road and walked back into Worthing centre. Our first stop was in Millets, where I bought a new pair of walking shoes. Then Angela bought some seeds and a bird feeder, while I bought Dad some more (six packs) Werthers Originals. We then had a cup of tea in M&S before walking back to the car and home.

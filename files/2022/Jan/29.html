Saturday 29th January

Quite a busy day, doing odds and ends, following our usual walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper. It was warmer than it has been, with occasional sunny spells, in spite of having rained over night.

Norman Wong had asked me to verify his identity for his application to become a STEM Ambassador. So I responded to the email, but need to view his documents. Teams insisted on wanting an update, so I asked him to organise a meeting. I also fixed a new bird table/feeder to the wall in the walled garden.

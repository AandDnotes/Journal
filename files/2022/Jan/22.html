Saturday 22nd January

A grey and chilly, but calm and dry day. Being Saturday we walked to the High Street to buy a newspaper, along with some spinach and rolls. Then it was the usual reading of the paper and doing the weekly accounts. I also had a number of Coventry Building Society letters to file away, having set up new regular savings accounts and moved money around.

After lunch we spoke with Austin, Juniper and Victoria. The former two in a somewhat sporadic fashion and then the usual mainly monologue from Victoria. It was -10 degrees Celcius in Berkleigh Heights and progress on their bathrooms had been slow. Builders were sitting in their van waiting for plasterboard to be delivered.

We managed to finish just in time to put on shoes and go to Felicity and John's for a cup of tea. We had a pleasant couple of hours chatting with them. Although their house is essentially the same as ours, being a mirror image makes it feel quite different.

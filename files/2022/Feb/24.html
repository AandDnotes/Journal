Thursday 24th February

This was the day that Russia invaded Ukraine!

Quite a busy day, with rain (heavy at times) before we set off for the supermarket. We went to Waitrose and then on to Victoria Grand to visit Dad. We had taken a flask of coffee, so sat in the car while waiting for our test to 'develop' and drank it. He seemed well, but had lost two pairs of trousers!

After we got home we had a hailstorm.

I did more research on Institutes of Technology and started drafting a reply to Greg. I also produced a start slide and speaker introduction for Tuesday's talk.

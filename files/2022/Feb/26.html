Saturday 26th February

We got up half an hour later than usual this morning, but then walked to the High Street to buy a newspaper, before continuing around part of the Rifle Range. We went out just before ten and got home just after eleven. It was sunny, with just a light breeze, so felt like Spring. In fact, it stayed like that all day. The rest of the morning was spent reading the paper, mainly about the situation in Ukraine.

This afternoon I did the weekly accounts, including a reconciliation against a bank statement. I also watched the live streaming of the FLL UK final in Harrogate (most of which was obviously not live).

Friday 25th March

The day did not start that well, with a piece of one of my teeth broke off while eating toast. Then wee took a walk down to the High Street to visit Chalcraft, where we finalised our funeral plans.

A little later Angela went to help Sandie decorate the church. After lunch we went off to Squires garden centre to buy a water feature that we had seen advertised. It will stay in its box until the walled garden is re-worked. Then I mailed Paul to suggest when we could visit them in May.

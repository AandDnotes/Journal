Saturday 30th April

Today would have been Mum's birthday and it was another busy one. Angela set off early for the church, ladened with cakes and I followed a little later to buy a newspaper and then sell lunch tickets.

I came back around three and filled the TC's engine with oil. I turned the engine over with the starting handle to pump oil into the filter and then ran the engine for a while. The oil pressure was good and there does not appear to be any leak around the filter bowl. However, I need to take it for a reasonable run.

Later in the day we spoke with Victoria.

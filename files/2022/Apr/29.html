Friday 29th April

Another busy day. Angela went down to the High Street to post Austin's birthday card and, after sending a couple of IET-related email, I fitted the water diverter in the down pipe on the front to the repositioned water butt. Angela also pruned a bush in the walled garden.

Hopefully I can fill the engine with fresh oil tomorrow afternoon (and keep my fingers crossed).

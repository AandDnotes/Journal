Saturday 3rd September

Another morning spent shopping. We walked to the Steyning market and then went off to Squires garden centre to buy the plants that Angela saw yesterday. That took a while, but we then thought we would pick some plumbs at Spring Gardens. We started with coffee and then walked around, but couldn't find any Victoria plumbs.

Needless to say, Angela then spent most of the afternoon in the garden. Meanwhile I did the weekly and the monthly account before reading some of the day's newspaper. Then I started putting together a web site on the cottage as part of my 'Reminiscences of an Ordinary Man' site.

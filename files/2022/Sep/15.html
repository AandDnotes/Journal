We started by going to Lyons Farm to do the weekly shop (and get a photo printed), before going on to Findon Garden Centre (mainly for coffee) and then to Squires.

Since the long spring on the accelerator linkage was rubbing on the link between the two carbs, I reversed the body bracket and added a 'solder tag' to the top fitting. I also did some adjustments to the slow running connection to the carburettors and then started the engine. However, I couldn't get the revs low enough. I think the slow running control is stopping this, so I started making a new bracket for holding the cable.

Thursday 1st September

The first day of meteorological Autumn. It was warm and humid, with a thunder storm in the evening (that interrupted the TV signal). We spent all morning shopping. After the window cleaner called we went off to Tesco/Dunelm/B&Q/Lidl/Tesco, with limited success.

After lunch we did bits and pieces, like finishing some writing. My second carburettor gasket set arrived, so I put the cork seals to soak in oil.

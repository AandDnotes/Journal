Saturday 1st October

We started by walking down to the Farmer's Market in the High Street. The only thing we came away with was a free crab apple tree. Well, a whip, which Angela put in a pot when we got home. We then bought two more whips, a rowan and another crab apple from the Woodland Trust. We will plant all three in the front garden when they arrive.

Searching for suitable whips (and tree protectors) took the rest of the morning. I then got down to the weekly accounts (including adding the analysis for the month) and also started the monthly accounts.

Wednesday 22nd June

Angela went off walking with her WI group and, earlier than was really necessary, I drove into Worthing. I parked in Grand Avenue and caught the bus back to the library. Being early I stopped for a coffee, then went to the Registrar's office. I was seen early and left at the time my appointment was due to start, with four death certificates. On the way home I dropped the 'green form' in to the funeral directors.

During the afternoon Sam, the celebrant, called. We opened a Zoom call with Alan and talked through the programme. We told her the music we had chosen and later she sent some suggested readings for use if we have time.

Afterwards we filled the car with stuff to take to the tip and I put together the photos and sent them to the funeral director and read the poems that Sam sent.

Thursday 16th June

It was a hot, sunny day. We started the day by going to Dunelm to get some net curtain and garden chair cushions, before going on to Holmebush. I bought some new summer trousers before we had coffee in M&S. Then we did the shopping in Tesco. For some reason I couldn't do anything right and the online order trolleys kept getting in the way.

After dinner we booked places at the WSMGOC June pub lunch and tickets for the musical at Chichester in July.

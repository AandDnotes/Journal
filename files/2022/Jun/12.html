Sunday 12th June

It seems to have been a busy day. We showered and Angela went off to church. I finished making the posts for the front fence and gave one side of them a coat of stain/preservative. After lunch I gave the other side a coat.

I sorted through another bag of Dad's clothes. They were ones that I thought were only fit for the recycling bin and I was right. Later in the afternoon we spoke with Paul. He still seems intent on coming over for Dad's funeral, even though he could view the live stream.

Angela was busy in the garden and then made some 'bullet' biscuits after dinner. I phoned Dad's friend Paul this morning and Aunt Joan before dinner. She hopes to join online.

Saturday 26th November

It was almost a normal Saturday, and it was dry. We walked to the High Street to buy a newspaper and Angela went straight into the garden to clear leaves.

A little later I went into the garage to check the tyre pressures and start tidying up the wood that I had glued together. I realised that I could have improved what I had done by gluing them together in a different order.

We had intended speaking to Victoria as usual, but they went out in 'their' morning. Later, they were still out, so we postponed our chat until tomorrow afternoon.

I had another email from MOKiM, saying that they will 'apply' for a replacement hub. So I supplied my address. We will see what happens.

Tuesday 15th November

I spent part of the morning downloading, editing and printing an attendance list for the evening talk. Then we (Daniel Eastwood and I) forgot to get people to tick the boxes!

We had our main meal at lunchtime since I was going out in the evening (and there would be some food). Then Angela went to a WI committee meeting. I also spent a long time on the phone trying to get the Government Gateway to identify me. The response was of no help. Having failed to use my passport and driving licence I tried the other option, which only required selecting a few options, Which, together with my passport details satisfied the system.

It rained most of the day and, although it had stopped raining when I drove to Falmer, there was a lot of spray. We had 42 attendees. It took a while to find the food, which was quite a way from the lecture theatre. It was a good talk.

Thursday 24th November

It is Independence Day for Victoria et al. For us it was all about having lunch out to celebrate (a few days late) Dad's birthday. Chris and Alan arrived at about 11:30 after an uneventful drive from home. We went off to the Fish Factory in very blustery conditions where we had a good (if somewhat large) meal. It started raining just as we got back to the car. We were too full to do anything else this afternoon.

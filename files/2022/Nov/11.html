Friday 11th November

Armistice Day. We were actually in a shop at 11am, so missed the two minutes silence.

This morning we drove to the Horsham Park & Ride site and caught a bus into the town centre. We then spent most of the day buying bits and pieces, arriving home at four o'clock. Luckily it was dry and quite mild.

Before we left I replied to Daniel Eastwood about Tuesday's talk. Then just before dinner I had a conversation with Deshi about the talk.

Friday 12th August

We both felt tired today. We don't know whether that is due to the heat or to Covid.

We moved the dining table back into the dining room this morning, after I had glued felt pads back on to some of the legs of some of the chairs. We also washed some of the floors. Angela did the cloakroom and hall and I did the dining room. It was too hot do anything else, although Angela went down to the Coop later in the day.

I phoned the owner of Victoria Grand, who had not found my email about Dad's bank account. She doesn't use either of the addresses that I had used! Later in the day I had an email from the probate office, saying that the will I had sent is not the original!

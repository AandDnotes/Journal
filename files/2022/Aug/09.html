Tuesday 9th August

Another day of wall-to-wall sunshine and temperatures of around 27 degrees. We set off fairly early, for us, and took the children to Shoreham Fort and on to the beach. They had a good time collecting stones and shells and paddling. Even Angela and I had a little paddle. The stones were very hard to walk on.

We then had coffee/ice cream at the 'kiosk' before coming home for lunch. However, we had to stop at Lidl in Shoreham to buy some things for lunch.

It was too hot to do anything during the afternoon, so the children played computer games and we read. Victoria and Brandon started another, oval jigsaw puzzle and I paid the window cleaners and returned Dad's over-paid Attendance Allowance. Then Victoria and I went down to the High Street to get some fish and chips, after which we were too full to do anything.

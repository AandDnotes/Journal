Thursday 11th August

We were up early so as to get Victoria et al to Gatwick by ten o'clock. We dropped them off and later paid the 'drop-off' fee online. We were home by eleven.

After lunch we took the hire car back to Littlehampton. When we got home we were able to relax a little, although Angela did some more washing and ironing.

It was another hot day, being the first of a heat warning alert.

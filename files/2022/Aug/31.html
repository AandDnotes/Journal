Wednesday 31st August

Our morning involved walking for both of us. Angela went for a long walk with her WI group up to Henfield (and then took the bus back) whereas I only walked to the 'cows field' to pick blackberries and then along the bypass and through Clays Field. I picked up a couple of cooking apples from the house on the Bostal, so I was out for less than an hour. The rest of my morning was spent reading (newspaper and email).

Angela spent the afternoon in the garden, cutting the two rear lawns whilst there. I sorted out Dad's bits and pieces that had been sat in a couple of paper carrier bags in the study. I found that both carriage clocks worked, if they had new batteries, and gave three magnifying glasses an initial clean. I sent a photo of the clocks and the brass pot to Alan to see if he wants any of them.

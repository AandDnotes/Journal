Thursday 28th July

Once again we were the only ones up early this morning. Before an early lunch Angela and I went down to the High Street to buy a few things.

After lunch we drove down to Shoreham and walked by the house boats, then across the glass footbridge and back to the playground behind the recreation ground car park.

After an enormous salad we packed up for the morning. Although we don't need to leave until ten, that is likely to be quite a struggle. Last night the children were still talking when we went to bed (and well after Victoria and Brandon went to bed). It sounds as though that will be repeated tonight.

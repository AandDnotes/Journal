Sunday 31st July

We started by walking down to Waitrose to get some things. It was quite a walk back up the hill. Everyone was up by the time we got back, so we went off to Llanfair PG to look for some waterproof clothing for various people. We had lunch whilst there and then went to LLangefni to look again and to buy food for dinner.

Paul et al had been sending messages and arrived just after we had eaten, but they had not. So they cooked some eggs. A little later they all went for a walk to a playground.

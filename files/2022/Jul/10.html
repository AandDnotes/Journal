Sunday 10th July

We set off for Chichester just after eleven and found the traffic to be heavier than we expected. We were not the last to arrive, but close. We then spent a couple of hours going up and down the canal, eating fish and chips on the way. It was another hot day, but there was a good breeze on the boat.

We got home at four, but found that Paul et al had gone to a barbecue at a neighbour's house. So it was about seven before we had a chat with him.

Saturday 16th July

Not a great deal happened today. It was sunny from dawn to dusk, so we walked to the High Street early, before it got too hot. We bought a few things in the Coop, including a newspaper. So quite a lot of time was spent reading it.

I spent the rest of the morning doing the weekly accounts, including reconciliation against the statement. I also received details of what Alan paid for the buffet at Dad's funeral, so moved that amount into his account from Dad's account.

The afternoon was quiet, until we spoke with Victoria. Austin had a good week at 'inventor camp' and both he and Juniper had bought suitcases for their holiday. Before that we booked a table at a pub in Chichester before going to the musical at Chichester Theatre.

For dinner, Angela cooked an interesting flaky pastry cheese and tomato quiche.

Friday 6th May

We continued our outings today by going to Borde Hill gardens, just north of Haywards Heath. We arrived in time to have coffee, then walked around the garden and a little of the woodland before having a sandwich for lunch.

It was a sunny, warm day (we took off our sweaters), although it clouded over later in the afternoon. We got home at about 3:30. I prepared the start slide for Tuesday's talk and sent it to Mike, explaining what we had done about the hybrid event.

Before we went off this morning I had an email from Alan to say that he was unwell and had tested positive for Covid-19! So they were not able to come down today to visit Dad.

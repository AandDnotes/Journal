Tuesday 24th May

We had mainly dry morning, with thunder storms just after lunch. So Lester 1 decided that he couldn't do any more. He will try to finish it off while we are away. He did ask, however, that we gave him a part payment. Because Lester was laying slabs around the back gate and in the front, we could only go out via the garage.

I spent some time uploading our Covid documents to the BA site and, once they had been verified, checked in and printed our boarding cards.

Angela walked down to the High Street after lunch to get some milk and we put out (and later packed) things to take. Then after an early dinner I joined the last Sussex Network talk as backup. The talk was a little disappointing.

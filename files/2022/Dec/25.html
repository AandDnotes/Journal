Sunday 25th December

A wet Christmas Day in Berkenwoude. So we didn't go out. In fact, the ponies came in early.

For dinner (in the evening) we had a 'cook your own pieces of meat on a hotplate' meal. We cooked peppers and mushrooms as well and had satay sauce and bread. Greet and Wim joined us during the afternoon and for dinner. In fact it was their hotplate.

We played games during the day and after dinner. We also had a quick chat with Victoria. They managed to get through to Kansas City yesterday, having met some road closures and following gritters.

Journal

This site is simply a record of our day-to-day activities following retirement, mainly mundane, but also providing the opportunity to comment on each day. It is intended only to be read by me; a sort of aid memoir to help my poor memory.

I set up this format in 2018, so years prior to that have one page per month. From 2018 onwards there is one page per day. There are few photos with us both and usually those have other people. This one was taken in January 2007 at Victoria's and Brandon's wedding in Gainesville, Florida.

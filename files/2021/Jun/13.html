Sunday 13th June

A hot and sunny day, although the thermometer only reached about 22 degrees. Angela went to church and I removed parts of the wall plugs behind the radiator int he cloakroom and then washed down the ceiling and walls with sugar soap.

Apart from talking with Paul during the afternoon, I mainly read the newspaper and Angela sorted out her WI accounts. We were just starting our main course (about 7:30) when the power went off. Luckily it came on again at five to seven, so we were able to watch the only TV programme that we wanted to see.

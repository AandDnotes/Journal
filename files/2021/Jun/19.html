Saturday 19th June

A more productive day. Between us we:

  • Walked to the Beeding newsagents to buy a paper, returning via the river bank, the Downs Link and the 'cows field'.
  • I did the weekly accounts.
  • And checked that NS&I had paid what they stated and that those figures were correct. They say that they have paid too much into Dad's account.
  • Angela removed bulbs from one of the big pots.
  • I undercoated the inside of the cloakroom door and primed some bare plaster.
  • We spoke with Victoria, quite late in the day.

The temperature only got up to 15 degrees and it rained a couple of times. Luckily we missed it.

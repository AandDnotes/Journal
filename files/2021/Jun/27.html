Sunday 27th June

Another painting day, hopefully the last. While Angela went off to church I painted around the edges, just finishing before lunch, and then used the roller on the rest of the walls after lunch.

It was dry, but not sunny, early in the morning. Then there were a couple of showers and the rain set in later. Later in the afternoon we spoke to Paul and Rowan, just before they watched the Netherlands play football (they lost).

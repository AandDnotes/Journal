Wednesday 23rd June

Angela started by going walking with her WI group. They went to see the pigs! I read through the FLL rules and missions and printed the score sheets. I also phoned Dad to see how he was. Luckily he seems to have 'completely' recovered from his urinary infection. Then I started painting around the edges of the ceiling and walls in the cloakroom.

After lunch Angela cut the front grass and pruned most of the bay bush in the front. Once I had finished putting the first coat of emulsion around the edges I cut both rear lawns and cut the top of the bay bush, while Angela cooked dinner and some biscuits. As we were finishing dinner I got an email from Rowan, asking if she could spend some of her birthday voucher on Minecraft. Of course, we said yes.

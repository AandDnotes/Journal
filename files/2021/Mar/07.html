Sunday 7th March

Another weather report! Cold, sunny, with light winds. So after lunch we went for a walk around the lower horseshoe. It was muddier than we had expected, but it was sunny and not too cold, even though the temperature remained at about six degrees.

This morning, while Angela attended her Zoom church service, I finished my computer basics 'how to'. I also read the SRFC newsletter that I had not received earlier. Peter Plank and Clive Upperton had each written several articles on planes they had built over lockdown. I also discovered that I started building my Tiger Moth five years ago! And that SMC an 'order and collect' service, even though the shop is closed. So I could get some CA and continue with the build.

After returning from our walk we spoke with Paul (and a little with Rowan). Their week had been as exciting as ours.

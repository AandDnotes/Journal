Thursday 18th November

Angela went off this morning on her own to do the weekly groceries shop. I spent most of the time she was out creating some of my November newsletter. Without many events I'm having to make up some parts.

Later I lifted the front wheels of the TC off the ground and checked the tightness of the cotter pin and the damper on the near-side. Angela was off again, this time to the butchers in the High Street. She then baked a cake for the Christmas fair on Saturday.

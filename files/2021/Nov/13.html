Saturday 13th November

Being Saturday we took our usual walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper and some rolls. It was cloudy, but calm and reasonably warm. As usual, the rest of the morning was mainly spent reading the paper, although I did a little more collection of images for the headlight and handle quiz.

After lunch and our usual attempt at a couple of Dutch lessons I put up the two light-weight brackets; one to the fence in the kitchen garden and the other to the wall in the walled garden.

Victoria was busy, so we agreed to talk tomorrow.

Saturday 6th November

We stared with our usual walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper. We also bought some rolls and walked through the farmer's market, where we found them collecting recycled material. So Angela walked back down later with some of our collection.

Otherwise it was the usual weekly accounts and a talk with Victoria a little later. Although I did replace the tape on the flex of the iron. There were a few fireworks last night, but tonight there were a lot of 'bangs' from next-door-but-one.

Wednesday 19th May

We seem to have had a busy day, but I'm not sure that we have achieved very much. We woke a little later than intended. Luckily we dressed before breakfast, since the plumber arrived while we were still eating breakfast.

Angela went off walking with her WI group at 9:30 and the plumber (with his helper) had finished before eleven. He will be back early tomorrow. I arranged for the recordings of our last two IET talks to be uploaded to our OneDrive and I edited the earlier one. Then exported it and ran it through Handbrake to reduce its size.

We spent part of the afternoon in the front garden. I cleared some more weeds from the drive and then clipped a couple of bushes that hang over the pavement. Then we paid the balance of the bill to Leaneys, so that the parts for the cloakroom can be delivered on Friday.

We didn't have time for a shower this morning, so we had one this evening.

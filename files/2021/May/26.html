Wednesday 26th May

We had a more relaxed morning, since the plumber was not due to arrive until about midday. So we had breakfast and then showered. In fact I was still in the bathroom (cutting my hair) when Angela left to join her WI walking group. They went up Sopers Lane and around the top horseshoe.

Another letter arrived from Coventry BS about telephone passwords. I'm not quite sure how that works, but I found that if I log in using my details (I already have savings account with them) I found that I could view Dad's account. And the £10 that I transferred Monday evening was there. So I then moved a further £39,990.

I ordered a slow running arm spring and cable clamp and two bolts to hold float chambers to carburettors. I looked for material to make the clips and bracket, but could find where I had put my off-cuts of sheet metal. I also cleaned the paintwork on the TC and put on some wax. I still have to clean under the wings.

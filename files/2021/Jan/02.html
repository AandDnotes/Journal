Saturday 2nd January

Today we got back to some sort of normal routine. We headed down to the High Street to buy a newspaper and some rolls. While I bought those, Angela queued at the Post Office to post a card. It was very cold and overcast, but calm.

After a while reading the paper I updated the weekly accounts and, after lunch, the monthly accounts. Between the two we spoke with Victoria. They have paid the deposit on the house they are trying to buy.

Just as I was finishing the monthly accounts I had a bit of a scare. It started with a slight chest pain that I felt was an indigestion-type pain. But then I felt sick and yawned a lot. I started sweating, but felt cold. Then my sight went blurred. According to Angela I looked very white. This lasted for about 20 minutes, I recovered after sitting in my armchair for a while.

Just before dinner I felt much better and prepared the starter; then enjoyed a splendid steak pie that Angela had cooked.

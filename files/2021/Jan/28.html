It seems that the easy access account, into which they put our matured regular savings account, can only be operated from a branch! However, they said that we could telephone or message instructions. After lunch Angela made a bread pudding (in addition to dinner) while I went into the garage and started the TC. I let it run for 15 minutes and checked things like the lights, including putting the speedo panel light bulb back in the hole in the back of the unit.

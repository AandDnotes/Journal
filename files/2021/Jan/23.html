Saturday 23rd January

A dry, if somewhat chilly morning. So I put on long pants before we walked to the High Street. We kept to the pavements since it is still very wet underfoot. While I bought a newspaper and some rolls, Angela went into the Coop and came out with quite a heavy bag. In addition to her list she had seen a wine that we like on offer. Before lunch I tried to sort out my python code, but got no further.

After lunch we spoke with Victoria. They are expecting to take ownership of their new house on Friday, after which they will spend a few weeks moving over. Then Angela went for another walk while I did my Dutch lesson. Then I again 'played' with my python code, but still got no further.

Friday 22nd January

Angela had left a few things off her shopping list yesterday, so walked back to Hyde Square, via the road this time. Luckily it was calm and sunny. Before she left I phoned Victoria Grand, having failed to get an answer yesterday. It seems that they all had the first part of their COVID-19 vaccination yesterday.

While Angela was out I researched a number of things; the shed we may buy, how we get our British Gas heating insurance to continue and video resolutions.

After lunch I fixed three horizontal wires to the new fence, putting screws in line with the bars. Then I experimented with searching for a usb port to which a micro:bit is attached.

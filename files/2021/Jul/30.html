I then tested the ethernet port on the MacBook adapter by connecting directly to the raspberry pi.

Angela spent quite a lot of the afternoon cooking. She made some 'chewy bars' as well as a casserole for dinner. Meanwhile, I answered a couple of email and then played with my 'pong' code. I managed to get the ball, instantiated from a class, to move by pressing a button.

Thursday 15th July

Angela went off this morning to do the weekly grocery shop. I noted the details of our planned trip to Kew and printed our tickets. Then I went into the garage and started replacing the nuts that hold on the exhaust, although I only replaced one before lunch. So I did the other two in the afternoon.

Prior to doing so, we laid canes in the walled garden to help visualise my latest potential design. Angela is not convinced and made several detailed criticisms. Later I experimented with some 3-D 'garden' design software, that was actually a only capable of designing rooms.

In the evening we watched 'The Importance of Being Ernest' on the TV.

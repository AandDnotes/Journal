Friday 9th July

Another tiring day back at St Lawrence Primary School. But another very rewarding day. We were with year 5 today, three classes, and they were very good. In fact, the winner of the year 5 race beat the year 6 winner from yesterday. The noise of cheering in the swimming pool was even louder than yesterday.

I got home just before four o'clock and, after a cup of tea, phoned Dad.

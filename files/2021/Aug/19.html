Thursday 19th August

Another busy day. I started by taking a lateral flow test before breakfast and then went to the dentist to have my broken tooth 'repaired'. The hole was filled, but is now 80% filling! If any more breaks off then I will need a crown.

I was home by 9:30 and we then went off to Worthing. I dropped Angela at Sainsbury's and then went off to see Dad. He was well and was still wearing his new glasses. On the way back to Sainsbury's I called into Argos to buy a shelving unit for the garden store. Angela had finished shopping and had had coffee. I waited until we were home.

After lunch we started moving things into the garden store. We also sorted out things to take to the tip tomorrow and put them in the back of the Panda.

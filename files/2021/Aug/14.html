Saturday 14th August

The building after the painting. While Angela walked to the High Street, I started building the new garden store. My new drill/screwdriver worked very well. It would have been very difficult with a hand screwdriver. We managed to rework the end of the raised flower bed and then put the four main panels together, along with the top and bottom front rails. I also made the missing block that is one of those used to fix the roof to the walls.

Later in the day we spoke with Victoria, deciding to move our weekly chat to after dinner.

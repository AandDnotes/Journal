Wednesday 18th August

A quieter day. Angela went off walking with her WI group, catching the bus to Shoreham and then walking back. I, meanwhile, finished the garden store. I moved the short piece of wood under the door batten to the door edge and turned the bolt upside down so that the screw holes lined up. Later I entered a review; 6/10, although we are pleased with the end result.

After lunch Angela emptied some of the shed and we tried the shelves in the store. We decided that it was too big and so decided to get a smaller set of shelves. However, I did post a photo of the store on Google Photos.

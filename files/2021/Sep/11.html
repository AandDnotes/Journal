Saturday 11th September

We were not very early this morning. So by the time that we had walked to the High Street to buy a newspaper and I had done the weekly accounts (including a reconciliation against the statement) it was lunchtime.

After lunch I started the TC. It took quite a few turns before any cylinders fired, but once going it seemed ok. So I took the car for a short circuit down Clays Hill, along the bypass and back through Steyning.

Later we spoke with Victoria. The children were playing next door, so we didn't see them. Brandon is unhappy with the management at work, which is not unusual.

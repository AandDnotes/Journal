Saturday 4th September

As usual on a Saturday we started with a walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper and a few items of grocery. It was noticeably warmer, but still quite cloudy until the afternoon. Being the first Saturday of the month it was market day.

On our return I checked the TC's battery. The voltage looked healthy and it started the engine easily. So I put it back on charge for the rest of the day. The weekly accounts did not take long, so I replied to our Twitter man and then added the currently scheduled Sussex events to my joint programme document.

After dinner we spoke to Victoria. The heavy rain of last week had passed and it was now a warm, sunny day. Brandon had some friends from work round, for I don't know what, and the children were left to their own devices. Their games seem to be more suited to four-year-olds. And Juniper looked particularly fat.

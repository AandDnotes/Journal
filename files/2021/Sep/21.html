Tuesday 21st September

The Autumn equinox, although it didn't seem like it. Today, after some mist, was mainly sunny and quite warm.

Angela started by taking her WI cash to the bank in Storrington; on the bus. I cleaned the front door bell push, so that it now rings, and fitted the two new smoke alarms. Then I carved out a piece of MDF and tried to re-dish the 'dished washer' from the off-side hub. It moved a bit, but the washer was stronger than the MDF, which started to squash.

After lunch I went to the RCEA AGM and talk at Field Place. The talk was on 'oddities' in the City of London. The lady was quite an entertaining speaker.

Later I had a call from Hamish about Ramon's need for a CEng application advisor.

Tuesday 27th April

Quite a busy day. I started by walking down to the solicitors in the High Street to collect the certified copies of Dad's LPA. I also bought some envelopes from the stationers. Then it was a case of putting the pack together to send to RCI bank. Angela also packaged the cardboard models for Austin and Juniper. We then went off to one of Angela's WI members in Beeding to deliver a booklet. We got back home just in time to prepare lunch, feeling quite worn out.

A little later we drove to Henfield, to the medical centre, to have our second Covid-19 vaccination. The rest of the time was spent dealing with email.

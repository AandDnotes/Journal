Angela went off fairly early to go walking with her WI friends. She got home just before lunch, having sat in someone's garden drinking coffee. My main job for the day was to draft this month's newsletter. It took me a little to clear other things, but I spent most of the day on it. I then got the Adestra system to send Greg a test copy for review. I also downloaded recordings of the last two talks, which will need trimming before uploading to our Youtube channel.

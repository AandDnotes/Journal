Saturday 10th April

In spite of Angela having a painful knee, she joined me on a short walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper. Later in the afternoon her knee was feeling much better.

I put the mower battery on charge before we went out so that I was able to cut the front and walled garden grass when we got back. Then, after coffee, and a quick charge, I was able to cut the kitchen garden grass just before it started raining.

After lunch we spoke with Victoria as usual on a Saturday.

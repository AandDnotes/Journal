Sunday 4th April

Another fairly immobile day, although my back does seem a little easier. The day started grey, windy and quite cold, although it become sunny later in the day.

After lunch we spoke with Victoria, finishing in time to put a half-shoulder of lamb in the oven, before talking with Paul. It was a good meal, the first roast (apart from Christmas dinner) that we had cooked in our little house.

After dinner we watched the Sky Arts broadcast of 'Jesus Chris Super Star'. Not quite what I had expected from an Andrew Lloyd Webber musical.

Thursday 22nd April

We managed to get out fairly early this morning. I dropped Angela at Lyons Farm to do the supermarket shop and I drove on to Victoria Grand (in the Panda this time) to collect the signed LPA and some mail. I then drove back to Lyons Farm and sat listening to the radio until Angela came out. We were home in time for morning coffee.

Greg had reviewed my draft newsletter and replied, so I finished and submitted it. Angela worked in the garden and later I clipped the bush at the bottom of the drive.

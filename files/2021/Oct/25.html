Monday 25th October

A more energetic day. Angela went to her 'exercise' group while I added a little to my article and started adding to my 'pong' game. By the end of the day I had added scoring and started putting in a serve button for each payer.

After lunch Angela went to give blood. However, the blood flow was slow and the nurse seemed to mess it up, so they gave up! Meanwhile I went for a walk; up the twitten, up the 'snake' path and then down to the bottom of the horseshoe path. I was only out for 45 minutes and Angela was home when I got back.

I was expecting a Eurotherm group chat at five, but then I realised that I hadn't received an invite. It seems that Colin had been in hospital, only returning home today, and so has organised a meeting for next Monday.

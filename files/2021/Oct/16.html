Saturday 16th October

From the weather forecast we didn't think it would drizzle and rain for much of the day. We set off this morning to the High street to buy a newspaper and some rolls and came home via the lower horseshoe path. It drizzled on and off for a lot of the way back and then rained heavily after we got home.

After reading some of the paper, I replied to my ESA man, forwarded details of his talk to Dave and replied to Marnie Middlemiss at Brighton about meeting for a chat, before doing the weekly accounts. I had to leave the reconciliation against the bank statement until after lunch, so it was quite late before I got into the garage. There, I started cutting up the remains of an old bolt to make a slightly thicker shim for the TC's near-side cotter pin.

Angela did some more to her patchwork cushion cover.

Thursday 15th June

We started by walking to a cafe that was part of a chain we had used in Davis. However, it appeared to be shut, so we walked around the corner to the 'Bean & Leaf'. It turned out that the first cafe's main entrance was around the corner, so it was probably open. However, the Bean & Leaf was very good. In fact, we went back there for some lunch.

We then walked along the 'green way' to South Station, deciding that we could walk that way in the morning with our cases. Having looked in the station we caught the T to Harvard and did a tour (self guided) around the old part of the campus.

Later we walked around the harbour. Quite an interesting walk.

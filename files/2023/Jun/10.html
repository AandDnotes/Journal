Saturday 10th June

Blue sky from sunrise to sunset, but much warmer and no sign of the forecast thunder storms. The water butts are nearly empty!

We walked (slowly) to the High Street this morning to buy a newspaper and some rolls and then Angela pottered in the garden while I did the weekly accounts. I had a bank statement to reconcile against my accounts, but ended up 45 pence short! In the end I just made a correction to my reconciliation sheet. It is where I include unclaimed payments and so is full of 'corrections' so as to make my accounts and what is in the bank actually balance.

After an early lunch I went off to the Repair Cafe in Beeding. In spite of the hot weather we were quite busy. Luckily there were plenty of repairers.

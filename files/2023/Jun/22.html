Thursday 22nd June

It seems that yesterday was the longest day of 2023, the summer solstice. Today seemed long because we were up before six o'clock, showered, breakfasted, packed and left the chalet by 9:30. We had decided to drive straight back to Berkley Heights and not try to visit anywhere en route. However, I kept my eyes closed for most of the way on the freeways due to Brandon's driving: Much too fast and much to close to the vehicle in front. Quite apart from the lane weaving to overtake slower vehicles.

We got back at eleven. After a lunch of re-heated Chinese food, Brandon went off to the 'beer shop' and then we all walked to the local park. Before dinner we started a jigsaw puzzle; a stylistic painting of New York city, from Central Park south. We continued during and after dinner, but didn't get very far.

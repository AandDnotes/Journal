Monday 5th June

Another day of unbroken sunshine and perhaps just a little warmer. Angela went to her exercise group and I walked down to the High Street to buy dollars, get some cash and find some rolls. In my light sweater I was quite warm by the time I got home.

The Saga we site would still not let me renew our home insurance, so I had to phone them and wait in the queue for ten minutes. But it was all done successfully. later I emailed Colin Cunningham to ask if he would run the PEI Group meeting next week.

Angela wanted to check that she had turned off everything at the church this morning, so we went for a walk, coming back via the cricket field. Again, quite warm in a light sweater.

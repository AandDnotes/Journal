Monday 21st August

The day started cloudy, but became sunnier after lunch. While Angela was at her regular Monday get together, the postman brought the washers for the TC's carburettors. So I fitted them (four per carburettor), putting Blue Hylomar on every surface and a spot of thread lock on the bolt. I started the car later and it showed no sign of a leak.

After lunch we drove to Spring Gardens and picked some Victoria plums and some runner beans. Angela washed, stoned and cut up some and put them in the freezer.

During the day I set up a web page and started transcribing my notes from my first trip to India. There is a long way to go!

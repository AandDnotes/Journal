Tuesday 8th August

We were all up just after seven and they were packed and away by nine. It seems that they had a good journey to Dover, arriving one and a half hours early. They were then home at about 6:45 our time.

We started by putting the study back together and putting the spare chair back in the loft. Angela did a couple of batches of washing, although it was drizzly for most of the day. She also did some cleaning.

Angela went to library and the High Street and my tablets finally arrived, so I walked to the pharmacy. However, I found that I did actually had one left (for Monday), so now I have to work that back into the sequence.

The house seems very quiet!

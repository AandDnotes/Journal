Just before eight o'clock I had a call from Dr Bennet. It seems that all my blood parameters are 'perfect'. He believes that there are two things going on. Firstly, a problem with my spine that is pressing on a nerve to make my whole leg go numb and secondly, a neurological problem causing the 'creeping' numbness in my feet/ankles. So he is referring me to the physiotherapist at the Health Centre to look at my spine and to the neurologists at Worthing to get a scan.

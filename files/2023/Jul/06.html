Thursday 6th July

Quite a busy day and a warm dry one. We set off fairly early (for us) to go to Lyons Farm to do the weekly shop. While Angela went into Sainsbury's, I went to B&Q to buy a tool bag and head torch. Neither were on display, but they were able to get them from stock on top of diplay racks. The tool bag is larger than I really wanted, but they didn't seem to have any knowledge of the smaller one.

After grocery shopping we drove to Spring Gardens, firstly for coffee and then to pick some fruit. We picked a large punnet of gooseberries and small punnets of raspberries and blackcurrants.

After lunch, while sorting out tools for Saturday's Repair Cafe, FEliocity and John came over for a cup of tea and a chat. Afterwards, I pruned back some plant growth coming over from Lynn's.

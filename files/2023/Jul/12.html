Wednesday 12th July

Angela went off with her walking group this morning. They caught the bus to Shoreham and walked back. A little later David F-B came and we set off in the TC for Warnham and the meeting of the Sussex Wanderers. We then went off on a run around some very twisty Surrey lanes. After lunch back at Warnham we drove back home, arriving at about 3:30. The TC did a total of 70 miles. We got just a little wet both on the way to and from Warnham, but the run itself was dry and quite sunny.

This afternoon Angela went to Tottington Manor for afternoon tea with friends from the table tennis club.

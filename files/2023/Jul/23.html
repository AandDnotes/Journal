Sunday 23rd July

A dry day today, although our usual Sunday format. Angela went off to church while I (unsuccessfully) tried to find out who is on our IET Sussex email account. I then went into the garage to work on the TC. It took some time to clean out under the car and replace some of the cardboard. Then I greased the steering, front suspension and prop shaft. I also checked the play in the steering box. It seemed fine, so I didn't need to play with the shims. Then I checked the fluid levels in the front dampers before putting the wheels back on.

After lunch we spoke with Victoria. Austen had just gone off for his scout camp. Victoria is joining him on Thursday.

During the evening we watched/listened to the PROMS performance of Beethoven's Ninth.

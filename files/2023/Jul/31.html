Monday 31st July

It rained until the late afternoon and then was sunny, but still breezy. Paul et al arrived just after 9pm, having driven through rain al the way through Belgium and France.

Angela went to her Monday morning meeting and I tried to get the TC's horn working, without success. After lunch we drove to Shoreham to buy a duvet for the spare bed, getting caught twice at the level crossing. At least we disposed of an old duvet. Then it was a case of converting the study into a bedroom for Rowan and making the spare bed. We had a little more time than expected before they arrived.

Wednesday 20th September

Dry this morning and very wet this afternoon, but windy all day. It was also a fairly productive day. I made updates to the IET Sussex home page and replied to a number of email. Then I managed to get the Repair Cafe iron apart and replace the cable with the longer one. Luckily I was then able to put it all back together. I did a PAT and tested it. Initially it made a few 'pops' when the thermostat turned off, but then seemed okay. It was probably some water flashing off. So I phoned the owner, who will collect it tomorrow.

Angela went to table tennis after lunch and later I had a Teams meeting with Dan, the new Sussex Network chair.

Just after eight the TV signal failed, probably due to heavy rain, so we switched to iPlayer to watch The Repair Shop. Then at about ten o'clock we had a short power cut. The power came back on just after we found the torch. Then it went off again at 10:35.

Wednesday 6th September

It was very hot today. I saw out outside thermometer show 28 degrees before lunchtime. Later, the sun was hazy and so not quite so hot.

This morning, while Angela walked to the High Street, I started cleaning the underside of the offside of the TC. I finished that later in the afternoon, while Angela was at table tennis, the first of the season. I later prepared some words for an advertisement to sell the TC.

After dinner I went to the Steyning Centre film night to see 'Living', with Bill Nighy. It was very slow (even slower than a normal Bill Nighy film), but quite a good film.

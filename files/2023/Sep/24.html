Sunday 24th September

Angela set off to church early, complete with trifle. It was harvest festival and, luckily for me, she brought half the trifle home. I started by oiling the garden table and chairs. It took less oil than I thought it would. Perhaps I didn't put enough on.

I spent the rest of the morning (apart from drinking coffee and reading the paper) generating the reminders to IET Sussex members about the first two talks. I sent them for approval after lunch, which was really about four days late! I also sent the posters (for the words) and the images to Mark and Dan for social media.

Later we had our usual conversation with Paul. We didn't see Rowan, only heard her.

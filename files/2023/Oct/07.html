Saturday 7th October

I had an early start, needing to be at the health centre by 8:30 for my flu jab. I bought a newspaper on the way back.

Angela worked hard in the garden, planting up pots for the winter. I did the weekly and then the monthly accounts, which took me most of the morning.

After lunch we spoke with Victoria, which seemed to take up most of the afternoon. We did spend a little time researching hearing aids, the expensive kind.

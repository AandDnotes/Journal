Tuesday 10th October

Quite a busy day and probably our last dry one for a while. I was up early and took the Panda to Simms for a service and an MoT test. I was able to collect it just before lunch so I didn't need to take the TC to Field Place for the RCEA talk.

That talk was given by Mike Underhill, mainly on the magnatron. His slides are more like lecture notes (lots of words) and those with a mechanical background found it difficult going.

Before lunch I started doing some research on how I could run a Windows programme on the Mac. The alternative seems to be running the program under Wine or installing a virtual machine with Windows.

Saturday 2nd December

The cold mist didn't really abate all day. And so it stayed cold all day, not getting above one degree all day. As usual on a Saturday we walked to the High Street to get a newspaper. Since a letter was delivered to us, instead of to Penlands Rise, we decided to go out again to deliver it, post a set of Christmas cards, get some cash and look around the Christmas craft fair at the Steyning Centre.

Angela went out again during the afternoon to buy some more stamps, while I did the weekly accounts. Otherwise it was a case of reading the paper.

Thursday 7th December

Today was all about Christmas lunch. That is, the RCEA Christmas lunch, taking place at the Findon Manor Hotel. We arrived at about midday and got home at 3:30. It was dry and reasonably warm on the way there, but very wet on the way home. We had quite a good lunch, although the outside of the roast potatoes were hard.

Before and after the meal we spent most of the day reading.

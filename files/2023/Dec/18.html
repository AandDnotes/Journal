Monday 18th December

I basically did nothing today. Whereas Angela went to her Monday club and later went to give blood. On a breezy, overcast day I stayed in doors.

We had our 'weekly' delivery of post today. There were about 30 items of post, so I had quite a lot of envelopes to open and a few letters/notes to read. Angela pinned up the cards on the 'Christmas' ribbons.

I read parts of the newspaper/magazine and things that came in the post. Angela was unable to give blood, since her iron level was too low.

Sunday 15th January

When we got up the rain had stopped and the sun was shining. In fact it stayed sunny until early afternoon when there was quite a heavy shower. It was a pity that the washing was still out! I turned the lights on and then half an hour later the sun was out again.

While Angela went to church I filled as may cracks around the house as I could find. That was more than I'd expected, and even then I missed one in the sitting room. It took me nearly all morning.

Later we spoke with Victoria and then with Paul. Earlier they had posted photos of their completed fencing to allow the ponies to wander into the shelter.

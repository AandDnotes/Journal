Sunday 26th November

A cold and grey morning, that became damp by lunchtime and raining by the evening. While Angela was at church I formatted the SD card for Mac, but after four hours Time Machine had backed up less than 2%. So I removed it from the list of backup devices and went back to the SSD drive. Then I sorted out the connectors for the Tiger Moth. Later I soldered the battery connector to the receiver and plugged in the servos. I created a new model, but couldn't get it to bind to the transmitter until (even later after some research) I set the model 'modulation type' to 'DSM2 only'. Then I could move the servos. I also prepared some PCB connector for soldering to the receiver and to the motor cables.

Paul et al were at Greet and Wim's for Wim's birthday, so we couldn't talk to him.

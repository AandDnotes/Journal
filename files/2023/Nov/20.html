Monday 20th November

Angela went off this morning to her Monday club, but before she went she remembered that 'collectables' were being bought at the Steyning Centre today. So I spent some time getting my Pentax camera out. I had to remove the leaking batteries in the flash.

So after lunch we walked down to the Steyning Centre with bags of silver plate, commemorative sovereigns and, of course, the Pentax. There were two assessors and after a short wait we saw one. The coins were only worth 25 pence each, which is what the banks will pay. He said that they could only offer a value of the plate based on their weight and that we would get a lot more from an antiques shop. So we got £5 for the coins and £15 for the camera. But we still have two teapots, a milk jug and a sugar bowl, that take up a lot of space.

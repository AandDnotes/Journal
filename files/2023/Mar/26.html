Sunday 26th March

After only about five hours sleep we got up for breakfast, finishing ours just as the 'gang' arrived for theirs. We left them and drove up to Northamptonshire to meet up with Kate and Dick for lunch. In spite of Google Maps taking us on a round-about route, we arrived at two minutes to twelve, with Kate and Dick arriving about ten minutes later. We had a good lunch, took a walk around the village (of Hellidon) and then drove back to Tillsworth for the night.

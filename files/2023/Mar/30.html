Thursday 30th March

Quite often on a Thursday we spend all morning shopping. So it was this morning. Although I did order two HDMI connectors, initiated a transfer to Victoria's account and gave Paul a birthday present, before we went out. We started by going to the Mayberry garden centre to get some vegetable seeds and some compost. Then it was coffee in M&S, followed by mainly vegetable shopping there and a few other things in Tesco. So were home in time for lunch.

While we were on our way between M&S and Tesco I got a call from the bank. When I did the online transfer, the bank's website said that a review would need to be carried out before the transfer was carried out. Phone reception was poor, but eventually the idiot fellow from the bank said he would approve the transfer. Later I emailed Victoria and she confirmed that the money had arrived in her account.

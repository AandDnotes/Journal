Wednesday 29th March

A grey day, with some drizzle in the morning and rain in the evening. Angela spent a lot of the day out of the house. She helped clear out the WI cupboard at the Steyning Centre before lunch and then went to table tennis in the afternoon. I spent a lot of the morning sending emails on various topics and also vacuumed the seats of the Panda, having 'washed' the driver's seat yesterday.

Just before two o'clock the car window repairer arrived to replace the Panda's windscreen. I had tried to repair the star crack, but the old resin I used would not go into the crack. That meant that the windscreen had to be replaced. I reversed the Panda out on to the drive next to his van and he had replaced the windscreen by 2:30.

I spent the remainder of the day trying various things to improve the performance of my hand-written number identifier; with little success. However, I did manage to get better contrast adjustment and filtering of the image. Although the content of the array looks clear, the neural network still gets some wrong.

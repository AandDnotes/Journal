Thursday 16th February

If success is measured in terms of the amount you spend, then today was quite successful. I started by filling the Panda with fuel at Tesco while Angela looked at dresses in Next. We then went to Lidl to do our weekly shop, followed by a trip to Dunelm to by new pillows and hand towels. We also bought two plastic baskets to 'contain' items on the shelf in the coat cupboard.

During the afternoon we spent even more. Actually we donated rather than spent, firstly to the DEC Turkey/Syria appeal, then to Turning Tides and raffle tickets from St Barnabas.

Later I researched outside lights and started developing a web site about the TC's fuel pump.

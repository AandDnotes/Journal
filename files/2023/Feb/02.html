Thursday 2nd January

We made a relatively early trip to Lyons Farm this morning and were back in time for a slightly late coffee. Then I updated the IET Sussex programme web page.

During the afternoon I refitted the fuel pump after tightening the screws. It worked well, without leaks, but there was a big leak from the banjo end of the new pipe between the carburettors. I discovered that the banjo has different sides, so I turned it round. It seemed to work, but I may need to use thread lock.

After dinner I joined a webinar on the Rolls Royce SMR. Little was said about the reactor design, but the description about the modularisation and construction was interesting.

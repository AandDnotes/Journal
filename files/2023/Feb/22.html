Wednesday 22nd February

We received our new general waste bin this morning. Luckily I put the old one out after breakfast (in my dressing gown), since they came before I was out of the shower. Later I fitted a hook on the lid for the chain to attach. I need to paint a number on the bin (as well as fix the lid on the garden waste bin).

Otherwise, I spent time preparing and sending forms to Dad's RCI and Santander building societies. I've now done all I can until I get replies from them. Angela went to table tennis in the afternoon.

After a hurried dinner I joined the IET Sussex events meeting. I think I only have a couple of actions!

Friday 24th February

The day was dictated by the fact that Angela had to collect her WI accounts books from the auditor in Angmering. So I dropped her at the house and went to what we think of as 'Roundstone garden centre', even though it is now called Haskins. I wandered around, had some coffee and an hour and a half after I had left her, Angela phoned to say that she was lost! She arrive 15 minutes later and then had coffee.

We decided to go into Worthing to look at chairs. We started with lunch in Waitrose and then went to Schadgels and Polands. We were home before three. Then it was a case of catching up with events.

Saturday 4th February

Not a very productive day in more sense of the word than one. I'd had difficulty going to the toilet for quite a few days and this morning I couldn't open my bowels at all. It was probably a side effect of the verapavil medication. So while Angela went to buy a newspaper and some rolls, I went see the pharmacist. She suggested a laxative, but is cautious about me being dependent on it. It hasn't worked yet!

The rest of the day was spent doing the the weekly and monthly accounts and reading the newspaper.

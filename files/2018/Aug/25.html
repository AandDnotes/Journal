Saturday 25th August

A quiet day, until the evening. I took Victoria, Austin and Juniper to Shoreham station, where they caught the train to London to meet Emily, Victoria's friend from UMIST.

So, on my return, I did the weekly accounts and when Angela had returned from Steyning, we spent the rest of the morning in the garden. I cut the front grass and after lunch started cutting the back grass (I only managed about half).

The train that they caught back from London arrived at Shoreham at six o'clock. So I met them at the station and we had dinner as soon as we got home. Afterwards should have been winding down to bed time, but it was a wind up to chaos!

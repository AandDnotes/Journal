Tuesday 29th May

An overcast day today, but still quite warm and humid. We went off this morning to Shoreham to park and catch the 700 bus into Brighton. We started with a stop for coffee before going to the probate office, where Angela swore her oath for the probate application for Bay's estate. It only took a few minutes and was done over the counter. After a walk to Churchill Square to visit Lakeland (about our leaking bread maker) we caught the bus back to Shoreham, getting home at about 1:15. After a late lunch Angela spent most of the afternoon in the garden while I wrote some IET email, updated my TC fuel sender unit web page with notes and photos of my recent work and typed some more pages of Dad's notes.

Friday 5th Octobr

We left home at a quarter past nine and headed north towards Badby. The M25 was easier than we had expected, but the traffic on the M40 was very heavy. After a stop for coffee at the first service station on the M40 we arrived at Badby at 12:45. After a late lunch we just had time for a quick walk around part of Badby. A light tea was followed by a couple of games of Rummikub.

Sunday 28th October

It was not quite so cold, but was overcast. Angela had a church meeting after the church service this morning, so was late home. I spent the morning doing updates on the Linux and Windows systems and backed up some files. Then I did a little more to the article for Totally T-type that I had started yesterday evening.

By the time that I had finished lunch (on my own) the sun had come out, so I went into the garage and started applying paint to the underside of the TC. I coated under the offside running board and the offside front wing. I'm undecided whether it will need a second coat, but I was really pleased with the colour match, even though it is in areas that are not directly seen against parts of the body top.

The clocks went back last night, so it got dark earlier. By the time that we had finished talking with Paul and Rowan it was getting very gloomy, but I only had my brush to clean. I then prepared the small images of talk posters and inserted them into my November newsletter.

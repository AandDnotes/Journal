Friday 12th October

Since we were not able to visit dad yesterday we started with a trip to Victoria Grand. Afterwards we went into Worthing to RBS (to pay in the cheque from Toovey's). We also bought some birthday cards and some egg cups for Victoria.

After lunch I submitted this month's newsletter for approval while Angela went to Steyning. Later I went into the garage, starting by repairing the doors and cleaning out the track. I then looked at the spark plugs, finding that they are still too sooty. Then I removed the forward carburetter. I dismantled it on the bench, ready to remove the jet.

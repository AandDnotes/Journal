Wednesday 31st October

Halloween! Since we are heading off to Rotterdam tomorrow morning we went to Victoria Grand this morning to visit Dad. From there we went to Sainsbury's to do a little groceries shopping. After lunch Angela went to table tennis and, after checking in for our flights tomorrow and printing off boarding cards, I went into the garage. I painted the off side of the foot ramp, gave the near side a second coat and touched up areas that I could not reach with a half-inch brush.

I then sent my article 'Introducing TC4985' to John James and later, apart from a visit from a little witch, we packed for the morning.

Monday 15th October

Being Monday I started by visiting Dad at Victoria Grand. Angela stayed at home to prepare for her WI meeting that she went to after lunch. I found a suitable bolt for the slow running cable, drilled a hole in it and fitted it to the car. Unfortunately, after running the engine for a while I decided to adjust the cable and the bolt broke on one side of the hole! I think that I over-tightened it. Lifting each piston in turn caused the revs to drop, which suggests that the mixture is too lean. Both mixture controls are set six flats from the top.

Just before dinner I spoke to the chair of the parish council about tomorrow's neighbourhood plan meeting. It is really just a planning meeting, so only worth going to the open meeting in November.

After dinner I created the survey for our business event and modified Greg's word for the email.

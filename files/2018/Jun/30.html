Saturday 30th June

The end of June already! Angela went off this morning alittle after 11 to Petworth House to meet her school friends, getting home just after six. Before she left I walked to the Beeding newsagents, then to the petrol station and then to Hyde Square to buy a paper. Before lunch I trimmed the large box bush. It was getting too hot by then, so I spent the early afternoon replying to IET email and doing the weekly accounts. Later in the afternoon I trimmed the yew bush next to the box and started removing its 'topnot'. I started clearing up just before Angela got home. After dinner I installed the latest version of Flash and cleared the browser cache (as suggested by All 4) in the hope that I could then get the end of Humans to run beyond the ads. It worked, but a Mac cleaner tool was also installed. So I removed it.

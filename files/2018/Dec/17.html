Monday 17th December

We arrived at Victoria Grand this morning early enough to get a cup of coffee. It would probably have been better not to have it! We didn't stay long since we wanted to go into Worthing to buy a case and we both had to go out early after lunch.

We went into Waitrose to park (and bought three things) and then really only went to Beals. There we found a medium-sized, blue, 'hard' case for a pound less than the one we had seen on the Argos web site. It turned out to be the cheap range from Antler.

After an early lunch Angela went to the WI Christmas meeting and I went to the dentist for a check-up. Luckily all was well. When I got home I went into the garage and fitted the steering column back into the car, including the rubber draft excluder. I then removed the top plate, wire brushed it and gave it a coat of black Hammerite. Hopefully on Wednesday I can fill the box with oil, re-fit the top plate, connect the drop arm and fit the steering wheel.

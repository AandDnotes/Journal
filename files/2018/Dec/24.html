Monday 24th December

We started with a trip to the tip in Broadwater, hoping to leave with them the diesel/petrol that I had removed from the TC's tank. However, they would only take it if it was in a container. So we brought it home. We did, however, dispose of some material.

From there we went to Waitrose to buy milk and bread (plus a few sprouts) and to have coffee before going to Victoria Grand. We were pleasantly surprised to find Dad in the conservatory taking part in some games.

After lunch Angela went around to Rosemary Avenue and I tried starting the TC. I could get it to run for a while with the choke out and the accelerator pressed, but it would not keep going. So I put the battery back on charge and will try to flush it through with petrol in the New Year.

Sunday 23rd September

While Angela went to church, in the rain, I did backups and updates and made notes for Monday's IET committee meeting. Then I went into the garage. I had intended refitting the valves, but realised that there was part of the head that I'd not painted and will be visible. So I gave that area two coats of red paint. Then I cleaned the exhaust and inlet manifolds (and 'clips') and sprayed them with aluminium paint.

The rain stopped at about two o'clock and the sun came out. However, it was still raining in Rotterdam, so we spoke with Paul. A little later we spoke with Victoria.

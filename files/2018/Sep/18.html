Tuesday 18th September

This morning Angela caught the bus to Storrington to tke her WI cash to the bank while I read the Brighton GOG documents. I then went into the garage and cleaned up and de-greased the thermostat housing and bypass pipe.

After lunch I went to Field Place, to the RCEA meeting. It was the AGM, which was short, followed by a talk on steam traction engine ploughing. On the way home I collected the new door key from Timpson.

When I got home I completed the September newsletter and asked Greg if I could send him a test version for review. While we were busy doing things, the 'for sale' board appeared on our front!

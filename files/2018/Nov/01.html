Thursday 1st November

We left at ten to nine and were at Gatwick airport south terminal by ten o'clock this morning to catch our flight to Schiphol. There, we had a little lunch before catching the train to Rotterdam, walking (in the rain) around the corner to Beurs and then the Metro to Capelsebrug, where Krista and Rowan met us. we were in Berkenwoude by about six.

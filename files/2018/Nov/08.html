Thursday 8th November

We were a little earlier than usual on a Thursday going to Victoria Grand to visit Dad. I'd seen him on Tuesday and shown him photos of our trip to Rotterdam, so there wasn't really a lot to talk about. From there we went on to do the weeks grocery shopping at Sainsbury's.

So we were home in time to have lunch at our normal time. My intent had been to put a second coat of paint on the foot ramp. However, it was a fine, sunny and quite warm day and the gutters were overflowing with leaves. Also, the coming days were forecast to be wet. So I spent most of the afternoon clearing out the gutters and valley gutters (as far as I can reach with a boat hook) and then 'fixed' the pipe into the water butt by the back door. I ended up just putting some duct tape around the top to join it back together. It probably won't last very long.

Then I checked some money transfers from Standard Life and from Equinity and sent an email to a young engineer who I had met last night and who works for Thales.

Friday 23rd March

I discovered this morning that I am the contact for the Eastbourne Big Bang event on Tuesday. So I had to hurriedly work out where we were going, what route we will take and how long it would take to get there. Meanwhile, Angela went to Steyning and to Rosemary Avenue. After lunch Angela worked in the front garden and, after cutting down a broken branch, I checked the TC over in preparation for trying to start it.

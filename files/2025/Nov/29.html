Saturday 29th November

A long and tiring day, at least for me. I left home just before 8:30 to drive to Mike W's. He then took me to Lewis Town Hall for the STEM fair. It was raining when I left home, but had stopped by the time we got to Lweis. The fair was very busy all day, even with four of us. I got home just after 4:30.

Angela had been to the High Street to buy a newspaper. When I got home we spoke with Victoria briefly (for her) since they are in Lees Summit and the girls were about to go off to see the Nutcracker.

To my surprise the parts I ordered from the MGOC arrived today.

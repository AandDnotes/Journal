Saturday 25th October

Quite a sunny, but cold day. As usual on a Saturday we walked to the High Street to buy a newspaper. Then I went in to the garage and painted the base of the mail box, along with the outer edge of the front panel.

We could get some free electricity today, so Angela put the washing on and we cleaned the oven between midday and three.

We spent quite a bit of the afternoon talking with Victoria. They should take posession of their new house next weekend.

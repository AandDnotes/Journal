Thursday 9th October

We had clear blue sky, with a minimal breeze, for nearly all the day. We started by going to Lyons Farm to do what was quite a small grocery shop. I also went to B&Q to get some pipe insulation to put on the pipe supplying the outside tap. We then went to Findon Vale for coffee.

After lunch we drove to the dermatology clinic at Haywards Heath. The doctor decided that the patch on my nose was not a sqameous carcinoma, but a solar keratosis, which is pre-cancerous. She froze the area using liquid nitrogen, which should remove the affected area. Patrick phoned just as we were about to leave. The link that I'd sent him to see a note of a Sussex Wanderers outing did not work. For some reason the # had been replaced with a % sign! So I told him to select 'Outings' and go to 2025.

When we got home I filled the back of the Panda with 'stuff' for our trip to the recycling centre at Shoreham in the morning.

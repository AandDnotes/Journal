Saturday 11th October

Following our usual walk to the High Street I painted over the area on the TC's chassis where the number is. Then I replaced the insulation on the pipe to the outside tap.

Afterwards I did the weekly accounts and reconciled my spreadsheet against the bank statement.

Then, after lunch I went to the Repair Cafe. It was busy at first, but then quietened down towards the end. I gave David F-B's donation and fixed an electric screwdriver. But, having got a blood pressure monitor to work, its screen did not work properly.

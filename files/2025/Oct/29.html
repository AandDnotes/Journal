Tuesday 29th October

Today was as wet as yesterday was sunny. Nevertheless Angela went walking with her friends, although they only walked to Truffle in the High Strret. However, I stayed in the dry. I collected together our set of regular donations in preparation for modifying them.

I then completed the report of my talk on SMRs for the RCEA newsletter and added a report on our visit to Shoreham power station. Then I did a little research on MGBs.

It stopped raining at about four.

Sunday 8th June

After yesterday evening's storm it was a relief to see dry roads and sunshine this morning. I had a tiring day. I left home at nine in the TC, drove to Beeding to get some petrol and then on to Burgess Hill to the annual car show, a distance of 16 miles. Meanwhile, Angela went to church and then later went down there again to cut back an errant rose.

There were many cars at the show and, since the weather was fine, lots of visitors. I looked at the assortment of cars, read some of my book and talked to onlookers (as well as a few members of the WSMGOC who were there). I put more petrol in the TC on my way back through Beeding so that I am prepared for The Wanderers run on Wednesday. The car ran well there and back. One person showed som interest in buying the car, but it was a tiring day.

Wednesday 4th June

While Angela went walking with her friends, I walked to the pharmacy, to be told that my prescription would be ready in 20 minutes. So I went to the Post Office to get some cash and then to the Steyning Centre to buy tickets for this evenings film. Then I went back to the pharmacy to be told that they had to order the tablets and should come back tomorrow!

I continued to try to find a way to parse the value sent by the MicroBlue app when the joystick is moved. Luckily I found a simple example that I implemented successfully later. So then I started wiring up a test of the motor controller chip that I have tried to repair.

After dinner walked to the Steyning Centre to see the film Conclave. It was slow, but quite tense and with a surprise ending.

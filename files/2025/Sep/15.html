Monday 15th September

Today was mainly about switching broadband from Virgin Media to BT. I started by walking to the Post Office to collect the BT router. A little later I had a message to say that we were connected, but the router would not connect. So I phoned the help line, who eventually said that the message was sent too early and that it should be connected before the end of the day.

Later in the afternoon another message said that everything was ready, but the still the router would not connect. So I phoned again. This time they booked an engineer to call on Wednesday morning.

Luckily the Virgin connection still worked for the day, although the phone stopped working during the afternoon.

Otherwise, Angela went to her Monday Club and I did the 'weekly' accounts. Later I ticked off the entries on the bank statement.

Later we went to the WSMGOC meeting at Washington. We had a meal there (half portions).

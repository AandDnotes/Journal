Wednesday 10th September

Angela went off early with her walking group wearing a full set of waterproofs. They walked down to Botolphs and back to the Hub. I was collected by Davis F-B and we drove off to Keymer to meet up with other members of the Sussex Wanderers. After coffee we followed the 25-mile route, which was good, returning to the Thatched Inn for lunch. Again I made a mistake in my meal selection. The chicken ceaser salad was not the best.

David and I left fairly early, encountering some torrential rain on the way.

After dinner I ran the PEI Group meeting, which went on and on!

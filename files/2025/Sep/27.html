Saturday 27th September

We started with our usual walk to the High Street to buy a newspaper.

I phoned Coventry Building Society and found that a transfer into my account would take two days! so I decided to use one of our Barclays accounts for which I can provide an IBAN code. I then spent quite a long time updating the weekly accounts and filling in the monthly account for the beginning of September (even though it is now near the end of the month). I had problems with two of the accounts, one of my own making (I used the wrong password) and one web site that just won't work.

After lunch we spoke with Victoria. They are now the owner of a house with a tenant (until the end of October).

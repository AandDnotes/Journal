Friday 14th February

Since I was out yesterday we went to Lyons Farm this morning to do our weekly grocery shop. I also bought some house numbers from B&Q to stick on our garden waste bin. From there we went to Findon Vale Garden Centre for coffee and to see how their building work was progressing.

The sun was shining intermittently, but improved after lunch, so we went for a walk before starting our Dutch lessons. I had some correspondence about the 'STEM in the Park' event in Crawley in May and also added a couple of notes to my 'fusion' talk slides.

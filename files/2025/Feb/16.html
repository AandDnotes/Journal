Sunday 16th February

Angela spent most of the morning at church and I looked at more on our trip to Canada. I realised that my cash-flow for the credit card was wrong, since each payment was for the previous month. So I could only buy travel insurance this month. I bought an annual insurance (which was cheaper than the single trip) from AllClear, who had 20% off, and was cheaper than the others that I'd looked at.

I then tried and failed to increase my credit limit. The decline notice said that I could try again in six months, so I will need to pay the balance for the Rocky Mountaineer using our debit card and wait to buy our flight from Vancouver until next month.

The afternoon was mainly spent reading the paper until we spoke with Paul.

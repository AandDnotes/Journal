Monday 3rd March

Today was a relatively early start to firstly pick up Peter W and then driving to Shoreham station to catch the train to London. The 'London walkers' (ten of us including Alan and Louis who I had not met before) then walked to Chelsea, walked along the King's Road, down to the Thames embankment, back up to the King's Road and then back to Victoria.

We couldn't walk through the Chelsea Hospital, except to go in to the cafe, where we had lunch, sat outside. At the end of our walk we had a meal in Wetherspoons in Victoria station before we caught the train home. I dropped Peter home and was home just before nine.

Thursday 27th March

Another fine day. We started by going to Lyons Farm (both Lidl and Sainsbury's) and then went on to Findon Vale Garden Centre for coffee and to look at plants. I also completed my draft of the last RCEA talk (mainly written by Mike W) and sent it to him.

Later I did some more measurements on the TC, this time the resistance of wires and of the coil in the petrol pump. I did some calculations and thought I now understood the measurements, but more thought made me unsure.

Before dinner I joined the CC-UK meeting. Then we had an interesting hard-boiled egg and fish finger caesar salad for dinner.

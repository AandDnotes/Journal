Friday 14th March

I had three main things to do today, sort out my repeat prescription, get on with the slide set for my SMR talk and get more done on the TC. I managed to progress all three.

So I still don't know how the system works. We also asked in the health centre if they had an appointment for an RSV jab, but they were all taken and they don't know when they will get the next batch.

Angela then did some cleaning and pottering while I worked on my slides. I put together those about the four contenders in the UK competition. There is then just the section on AMRs to do. However, I still have the animation to add and the talk is on Tuesday.

It was pretty messy, I hope with brake fluid rather than differential oil and it took me a while to get the handbrake linkage out.

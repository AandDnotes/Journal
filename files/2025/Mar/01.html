Saturday 1st March

The first day of meteorological Spring. And another fine, sunny, but chilly day.

We walked to the High Street to buy a newspaper and then I did the weekly and monthly accounts. Shortly after lunch Angela went to the Methodist church to help prepare for a St Barnabas 'beetle drive', along with a cream tea. I went down for the start and we got home just after five.

We then spoke with Victoria before we had a late dinner.

Monday 4th August

Everyone was up early so that Victoria could take us to NW Arkansas airport. Google maps took us by the scenic route (avoiding highways), which was interesting, but twisty. I don't think Juniper appreciated it.

So we got to Toronto via Atlanta and caught the shuttle bus to our hotel, which was not in a particularly good area. In fact, the hotel was directly under the flightpath of landing aircraft!

All we did was go out to a 'transport cafe' for dinner.

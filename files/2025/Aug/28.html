Thursday 28th August

We are back into our routine of doing the weekly shop on Thursday. So Angela drove to Lyons Farm, we started in Lidl, went to Sainsbury's to buy a few more things and then went on to Findon Vale for coffee, to find a small tree to replace one in the pot by the front door and to buy something for a Christmas present for Kate and Dick.

After lunch Angela finished clearing weeds from the drive, planted the new little tree and pottered in the garden.

I emailed the editor of the MG Octagon Club Bulletin about using Kurvier and then tried to get a photo on my Toronto web page to open a large version. But the link covers all the text, rather than just the photo.

These activities were interrupted by an email from Victoria to say that they've made an offer on a house (with a pool). So we spent some time looking at the photos.

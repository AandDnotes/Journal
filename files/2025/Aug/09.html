Saturday 9th August

I had a good night's sleep, but Angela is not feeling well. So I started by going to a nearby pharmacy to get her some imodium equivalent. She went back to bed and I went for breakfast.

Angela spent most of the day sleeping, although we did go for a couple of gentle walks. The major event was an email from the Post Office. So we went to a bank and reset Angela's money card. I went to a local cafe for some lunch.

On one walk (after the bank) we found the SkyTrain station that we will use on Monday to get to the airport. The other walk was to a nearby park, where there was a farmer's market. We also walked around some of the back-streets. After that we went into a supermarket and I bought a sandwich, a muffin and a nectarine.

When we got back to the hotel I went for a swim. Angela didn't eat anything all day, but seems to be improving. We tried watching some TV. but they are mostly advertisements.

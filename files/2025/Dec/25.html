Thudsday 25th December

Christmas Day, and a very sunny one. However, it was also very cold, with a biting wind, or so we were told. Luckily Angela and I didn't need to go out of the house.

We opened presents and then helped peel vegetables before lunch. Greet and Wim came for a while. We all had a good turkey and pork lunch and then they left.

Later we played a game and then watched a film (The Holiday).

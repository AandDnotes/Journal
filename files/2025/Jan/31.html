Friday 31st January

Then it was back to trying to sort out backups. The latest linux system would not show the data partition, but, luckily, a previous version would. So I backed up the remaining files, including an export of the Photos library. Now I just need to map out the partitions and delete those that I don't want to remain on the laptop when I give it away.

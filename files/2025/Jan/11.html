Saturday 11th January

The outside thermometer was indicating -3 degrees C this morning. However, it was calm and sunny. Later, it did reach the heady heights of zero.

We walked to the High Street to get things for the lunch club and a few things for us, including a newspaper.

I spent the rest of the morning doing the weekly accounts and reconciling the bank statement against my spreadsheet. It all balanced!

After lunch I went to the Repair Cafe. We were very busy. I looked at an electric blanket, some dinosaur lights, a toaster and a satellite TV receiver, the latter of which I brought home.

A message earlier from Victoria said that they were expecting their furniture to arrive today, so we may need to talk with them tomorrow.

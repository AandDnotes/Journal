Tuesday 15th April

Angela went off to Storrington on the bus to bank some of her Lunch Club cash. I spent some time researching the interface between an Arduino and a Bluetooth transceiver. Then I patched together enough to test two Lego motors. Much of the time was setting up a 9 volt supply. After lunch I used a 'step-down' module to convert 12V to 9V and used a potential divider to vary the speed of the motor. The next step is to use a motor driver.

Saturday 12th April

Another sunny and even warmer day. We were up a little later than usual and started by going to the High Street. I then did the weekly accounts while Angela cut the grass, since it might be the last chance before we go away.

After lunch it was time to go to the Repair Cafe. It was pretty quiet, even though there were only four of us on the electrical bench.

When I got home we spoke with Victoria. It seems that Brandon had a bad week at work. His view seems to be getting worse rather than better.

Thursday 3rd April

We started with our usual trip to the supermarket to do the weekly grocery shop. It's a pity that I forgot to take our grocery bags! While Angela started in Lidl, I went to B&Q to get a new hinge for the bin store and some lubricating oil (both 3-in-1 and spray). We then went to Boots before going on to Findon Vale for coffee.

After lunch Angela went around to Shiela and John's to help move some compost bags. I didn't see her for the rest of the afternoon! Meanwhile, I replaced the broken hinge on the bin store, finished the monthly accounts, printed the route instructions for next week's Wanderers run and started putting together the RCEA April newsletter.

Although there was a little high cloud this afternoon, it was another sunny day and the forecast is for the fine weather to last beyond the middle of next week. So I might be able to take the TC on the next Wanderers run.

Monday 28th April

I was up a little earlier than usual and drove to Shoreham to join Eurotherm friends to go to London. Angela went to her Monday club.

It was a gloriously warm and sunny day. We took the Tube to Hammersmith and walked west along the northern shore of the Thames. Our route diverted inland a little and we stopped for lunch at a cafe in the grounds of Chiswick House. Then it was on to Kew Pier. We sailed back to Westminster Pier, sitting on the top deck.

After a light meal at the usual pub we caught the usual train back. I got home at nine o'clock.

Sunday 13th April

The day was bright, but cloudy, with the occasional sunny spells. It became noticeably cooler as the day went on and the breeze picked up. While Angela went to church I put some primer on the garage step and later gave gave it two coats of stain.

I spent most of the morning updating the RCEA April newsletter. I must find a better way of keeping the images with the text, since it took a long time to re-position them. Later I added Mike W's amendments to the notes on Ray P's talk.

However, I did find time to look for some motors in the 'control technologies' boxes. In addition to some motors that would plug into the control boxes, I found some Lego motors and other Lego kit, including gears. I need to find out if I can connect these motors to an Arduino and battery box.

Later in the afternoon we spoke with Paul. Rowan had been to the cinema in Goude with a friend.

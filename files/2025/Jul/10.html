Thursday 10th July

Rather than our usual morning trip to do the weekly grocery shopping, Angela went off to a funeral, so I enlarged the holes in my strip-board so that the legs on the larger components will fit and wired a power jack to the leads from the battery case. I also sorted out a few Lego pieces to take with us.

After lunch (Angela had eaten scones at Squires with those she had gone to the funeral) We went off to do our weekly shop. We started by buying Angela a 'pack away' cagoule, then shopped in Waitrose and finally went to Lyons Farm. We bought mainly drinks in Sainsbury, then filled the Panda with fuel and then I bought some electrical solder in B&Q. To my surprise they didn't have any Terry spring clips, which I think might help hold a heat shield on to the fuel line that runs above the exhaust manifold on the TC. In the heat it was quite a tiring outing.

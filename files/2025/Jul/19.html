Saturday 19th July

We woke to heavy rain and an overflowing gutter. So we dressed in wellies, waterproof trousers and cagoules and walked to the High Street under umbrellas. We stayed dry, but sweated a lot. I then did the weekly accounts and wasted time looking at a means of creating an app on the iPad to interface via Bluetooth to the Arduino, only to find that I can't install an app on an iOS device without having an Apple Development licence (at a cost of $99).

Luckily the rain had stopped by lunchtime and there were sunny periods. I cleaned out the gutters and trimmed the top of the bay tree, while Angela pottered in the gardens.

A little later we spoke with Victoria and Brandon. They are in Fayetteville, expecting their furniture to arrive tomorrow. Brandon's first week in his new job seems to have gone well.

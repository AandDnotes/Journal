Friday 3rd August

On Wednesday I had ordered four tyres for the Panda. This morning I went into Worthing to get them fitted. While that was being done I walked to the RBS to pay in a cheque and have a coffee, before collecting the car and going to Victoria Grand to see Dad. I was home by lunch time. I sent that to Bernard and Greg for input. I also emailed Mike Teller, who wants to apply to become a Fellow.
